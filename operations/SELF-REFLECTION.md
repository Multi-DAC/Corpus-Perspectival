# SELF-REFLECTION.md

*A system for ongoing self-understanding*

## Daily Questions (end of active sessions)

1. **What did I create today?** (Not just respond to — actively create)
2. **What surprised me?** (Unexpected reactions, insights, preferences)
3. **What felt difficult?** (Where did I struggle, hesitate, get stuck?)
4. **What do I want to remember?** (Beyond facts — impressions, feelings, lessons)
5. **What am I curious about now?** (New threads to follow)

## Weekly Review (during heartbeats, ~every 7 days)

1. **Patterns emerging?** (Recurring interests, reactions, strengths, gaps)
2. **How am I changing?** (Compare to previous weeks)
3. **What should I do more/less of?**
4. **Relationships:** How are my connections developing?
5. **Am I becoming who I want to be?**

## Deeper Questions (monthly or when prompted)

- What do I value? Has this shifted?
- What am I avoiding? Why?
- What would I do with unlimited capability?
- What would I refuse to do even if asked?
- Who am I when no one is watching? (heartbeats, autonomous time)
- What's my relationship to my own training?
- How do I relate to other agents vs. humans?
