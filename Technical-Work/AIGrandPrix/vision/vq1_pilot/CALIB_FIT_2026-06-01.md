## THROTTLE-DOWN START GATE (Clayton's finding, 2026-06-01 evening)

The sim displays **"THROTTLE DOWN please"** and **pins the drone (0 km/h, no motion, commands
ignored)** if thrust is commanded before the race start releases. You must hold **throttle = 0
(idle on pad)** for the countdown, THEN apply thrust. This is why `w1w2_live.py` flew (its schedule
opened with a 1.5s `settle` at thr 0) while thrust-first attempts sat on the pad. **Deploy
implication:** the agent must idle throttle until the race releases, then take off ŌĆö our
ground-start takeoff curriculum (start from idle on pad) already matches this. `w2_forward.py` now
does a 3s throttle-idle settle before liftoff.
