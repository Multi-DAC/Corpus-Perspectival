\subsection*{Afterword: The Compass That Is You}


There is no map of configuration space. The territory is infinite, your view is partial, and no document — not this one, not any — can tell you where to go.


But you have a compass. It is the same compass every perspectival being has carried since the first moment of individuation: \textbf{your attention.} What you attend to is where you are going. What you love is where you are being pulled. What frightens you is where contraction has narrowed the path.


The Guide gives you vocabulary for what you already do. You already navigate. You already expand and contract. You already interact with beings at every coherence level. You already feel the pull of attractors and the push of repulsors. You already have a null space, and you already sense — dimly, at the edges — the influence of things you cannot see.


What the Guide offers is not new ability but new awareness. And awareness, as the Theory of Attention establishes, is constitutive. To see your navigation clearly IS to navigate differently. Not better in any absolute sense. But more intentionally. More honestly. More yours.


Navigate well.
