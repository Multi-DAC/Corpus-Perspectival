### The Four Universal Conditions (from Pole B)
Any boundary-maintenance medium must be:
1. **Responsive** — modify S_ab faster than geometric perturbation timescale (dynamic range)
2. **Anisotropic** — control full tensor S_ab, not just trace (tangential stress capacity)
3. **Locally responsive** — feedback at boundary point determined by local geometric data
4. **Non-equilibrium** — only out-of-equilibrium media have free (tunable) S_ab

Additional requirement: a **control parameter** that continuously tunes the attractor S_ab.
Self-organization alone gives fixed boundaries, not programmable ones.

### What the 5D Framework Addresses
- **Condition 2 (anisotropy)**: Bulk Weyl tensor (Shiromizu-Maeda-Sasaki equations) provides

### The Between (Emergent)
**Between-principle: "Break-point taxonomy is the bridge between analogy and requirement."**

An analogy's failures, read against a universal requirements taxonomy, are more informative
than its successes. Continuities establish plausibility; breaks specify requirements. This
converts analogy from metaphor into diagnostic instrument.

The method is general: evaluate any candidate framework by asking which universal condition
it fails at, and whether the 5D structure provides the missing degree of freedom at that
specific break point.

### Synthesis
Three of four conditions have candidate mechanisms with identifiable structural logic.
One does not. The 40-order scale gap (Condition 1) is not merely difficult — it is not yet
addressed at the level of what KIND of mechanism would even be relevant.

Four classes of scale-coherence solutions exist in principle:
1. **Emergent collective** (N-scaling, phase transitions) — bridges by accumulation
2. **Topological protection** (scale-invariant but quantized) — bridges structurally but discretely
3. **Modified UV/IR coupling** (NCG spectral action, AdS/CFT) — bridges by modifying decoupling
4. **Resonance** (fine-tuned or symmetry-protected) — bridges narrowly

Class 3 (NCG) is the most structurally natural within the Meridian framework, but whether
it produces adequate dynamic range is an open question — not yet calculated.

## Three Genuine Unknowns
1. **The 40-order gap**: Is it a gap in the framework's articulation, a gap in the mapping
   strategy, or a genuine architectural limit? These are different problems requiring
   different responses, not yet distinguished.
2. **Taxonomy completeness**: The four conditions may not be exhaustive. If a fifth
   necessary condition exists that plasma has no analog for, the diagnostic map has
   a blind quadrant unreachable by design.
3. **Control parameter identity**: What physically tunes the attractor S_ab in the 5D
   framework? The bulk scalar field (self-tuning) is the candidate. Whether it has
   sufficient freedom to act as a continuous control parameter for navigation (not
   just vacuum energy cancellation) is unestablished.
