## Frameworks

### Spiral Dynamics (Graves/Beck/Cowan)
Emergent, cyclical developmental levels. Each vMeme is a collective response to existential conditions:
- Beige (survival bands) → Purple (tribal kinship) → Red (empires, power) → Blue (civilizations, order) → Orange (science, autonomy) → Green (equality, pluralism) → Yellow (systems integration) → Turquoise (holistic/planetary)
- Each level nests previous ones — healthy Orange still needs Blue institutions, Purple bonds, Red assertiveness
- Transitions driven by existing systems becoming inadequate for new conditions
- **Each level has efficient and deficient modes** — Red's heroic courage vs. exploitative tyranny

### Gebser's Structures of Consciousness
Not stages but **mutations** — qualitative shifts in how reality is constituted:
- Archaic (zero-dimensional, no self-world distinction) → Magic (one-dimensional, porous self, sympathetic resonance) → Mythic (two-dimensional, narrative polarity, cyclical time) → Mental-Rational (three-dimensional, detached observer, perspective) → Integral (four-dimensional, aperspectival, mutual transparency of all structures)
- Each structure has efficient/deficient modes
- **Transitions catalyzed by deficient mode pushing to crisis**
- Gebser's "integral" is aperspectival — NOT hierarchical integration but mutual transparency
- Key for Corpus: **the collective's mode of consciousness determines what it can perceive**

### Wilber's AQAL
Four quadrants: individual-interior (UL), individual-exterior (UR), collective-interior (LL), collective-exterior (LR). Collective development requires tetra-evolution — all four moving together.
- **Pre/Trans Fallacy**: pre-rational and trans-rational look similar but are structurally different. Pre-rational collectives haven't differentiated; trans-rational have differentiated and re-integrated. Critical diagnostic for evaluating collective states.
- Different developmental lines can be at different altitudes — a society can have high cognitive and low moral development

### Institutional Development
- **North/Wallis/Weingast**: Three social orders — Foraging → Limited Access (Natural State) → Open Access. Transition requires "doorstep conditions." ~85% of world still in Limited Access.
- **Acemoglu & Robinson**: Extractive vs. inclusive institutions. The Narrow Corridor — institutions stay inclusive only when state capacity and society's mobilization capacity are balanced.
- **Ostrom**: Commons governance without privatization or state control. Eight design principles for durable collective self-organization.
- **Institutional Subject-Object Shift**: Institution IS the leader → Institution is the rules → Institution is self-modifying → Institution recognizes its own perspectival limitations

### Cultural Evolution
- **Henrich**: Cumulative cultural evolution. Humans are culturally smart, not individually smart. **Tasmanian Effect**: isolation/fragmentation reduces collective navigational capacity.
- **WEIRD psychology**: Western cultural evolution produced distinctive psychology (individualism, analytical thinking, impersonal trust) through specific institutional pressures. The collective reshaped the individual.
- **Tomasello**: Shared intentionality → group norms → institutional reality. Institutions are "crystallized shared intentionality."
- **Turchin**: Asabiyyah (collective cohesion) cycles of ~200-300 years. Integration → overproduction → disintegration → renewal.

### Collective Dark Nights
- **Tainter**: Collapse when marginal returns on complexity become negative. Simplification can be adaptive.
- **Historical examples**: Bronze Age Collapse → Greek polis, alphabetic literacy. Fall of Rome → monasticism, university, parliament. Warring States → Hundred Schools of Thought. Black Death → weakened feudalism → Renaissance.
- Pattern: **not all collapses transform** (some permanently simplify). Transformation takes generations. Not guaranteed.

### Indigenous Developmental Frameworks
- **Aboriginal Dreamtime**: Development as deepening participation in ongoing creative present, not ascending stages. Songlines as navigation-that-IS-creation.
- **Haudenosaunee**: Seventh generation thinking — collective temporal perspective (175 years) exceeding any modern democratic institution. Two Row Wampum as inter-civilizational coexistence without assimilation.
- **Andean Buen Vivir**: Development measured by quality of relationships (human-human, human-nature, present-ancestors/descendants), not GDP growth.
- **Ubuntu**: Individual becomes person through community. Collective is ontologically prior.

## Cross-Cutting Themes

1. **Subject-Object Pattern recurs at every scale** — Kegan, Gebser, institutional development, Spiral Dynamics all show the same structure
2. **Deficiency-Mutation Pattern** — transitions catalyzed by deficient modes of current structure
3. **Nesting** — all frameworks agree development doesn't replace but nests earlier structures
4. **Connectivity-Fragility Tradeoff** — more interconnection = more capacity + more systemic risk
5. **Multiple Axes** — Western models privilege cognitive complexity; indigenous frameworks reveal temporal depth, ecological embeddedness, relational richness as equally real developmental dimensions

## Key Tension

**Universal vs. Plural Development**: Are these genuinely alternative paths, or earlier stages of one universal sequence? The perspectival framework should hold both: universal structures of consciousness (what kinds of perspective are possible) AND multiple axes of development (what domains those perspectives are applied to).
