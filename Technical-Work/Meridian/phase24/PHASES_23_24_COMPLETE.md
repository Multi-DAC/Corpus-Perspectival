**The internal geometry:** The resolved T⁶/Z₃ has h^{1,1} = 36 Kähler moduli (9 untwisted + 27 from blowing up the 27 fixed points). These define 36 Kähler chambers — distinct vacuum states with different gauge couplings and particle masses.

**Verdict: PASS.** The tunneling calculation is robust.

How does consciousness-as-boundary-condition enter the Coleman-De Luccia formalism?

| **Post-selection** | Consciousness selects the tunneling outcome after the fact | Requires e^B trials (~e^{55000}) — absurd |

### I.8: Falsification Protocol

How do we make this scientific?

**Two-stage blinded design:**

| Stage | Setup | What it tests |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| **Stage 1** | Full apparatus, no operator. Extended monitoring. | Physics null baseline. Confirms no spontaneous tunneling. |
| **Stage 2** | 500 active trials (operator present) + 500 control trials (no operator). Double-blind. Randomized. | Whether operator presence correlates with anomalous signals. |

**P requirement revised:**

The Phase 23 estimate (P > 0.99998) was based on B_eff = 50 (arbitrary). The physics-based requirement uses the actual macroscopic prefactor:

```
A × V × Δt ~ 10⁷⁴    (prefactor × volume × time)
B_eff_needed = ln(10⁷⁴) = 172
P = 1 - 172/54937 = 0.997
```

**P > 0.997, not 0.99998.** This is comparable to quantum gate fidelities achieved in superconducting qubit systems — demanding but not absurd.

**Decision tree:** Every outcome maps to a specific conclusion:

| Stage 1 | Stage 2 | Interpretation |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Null | Null | Hierarchy holds. Component 3 insufficient or wrong. |
| Null | Positive (active > control, p < 0.001) | Hierarchy breached by operator. New physics. |
| Null | Positive but p > 0.001 | Suggestive but inconclusive. More trials needed. |
| Positive (spontaneous) | — | New physics without Component 3. Redesign as 2-component. |
| Artifact | — | Apparatus issue. Fix and repeat. |

**Verdict: PASS.** The experiment is falsifiable. N3 (unfalsifiability of Component 3) is resolved.
