## What This Book Is

A one-parameter theory of dark energy derived from two geometric axioms. 5D warped geometry (Randall-Sundrum orbifold) combined with noncommutative geometry (Connes spectral action) produces Einstein gravity, the Standard Model gauge sector, and a cuscuton dark energy equation of state from a single action principle. The volume presents this framework as a map of one attractor basin in configuration space — not a Theory of Everything, but a derivation of what follows from inhabiting this specific geometry.
