# Meridian Positioning: Against the Greene-Witten Landscape Assessment (2025)

## Source
Transcript of public conversation between Brian Greene and Edward Witten (~2025). 62 minutes covering string theory's state, open problems, observational prospects, cosmological constant, anthropic principle.

## Key Witten Quotes Relevant to Meridian

1. **Missing principles:** "The unifying principles behind [String Theory], in my opinion, are not known, and that's why we are still largely in the dark." (03:58)

2. **Imprecise real-world models:** "I don't think our models of the real world based on String Theory have gotten much more precise than they were in the early days." (28:04)

3. **Cosmological constant despair:** "I made my peace with [anthropics] because I had no alternative." (23:50)

4. **What would give him hope:** "I'd be incredibly happy if it was discovered experimentally that [the cosmological constant is constant in time] is false because I think that would give a better chance of a more conventional scientific explanation." (25:45)

5. **DESI specifically:** "There is actually an experiment, the DESI Collaboration that has claimed evidence in that direction. Unfortunately... I don't consider it convincing at the present time, but I'm ready to be convinced if anybody has better data." (26:38)
