They required a fundamentally new geometric insight — spacetime curvature — that Newtonian gravity couldn't contain.

The 12% has exactly this character. Fifteen mechanisms eliminated — Meridian's Le Verrier searches. Each one failed, and each failure sharpened the signpost.
