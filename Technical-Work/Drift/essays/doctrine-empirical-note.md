## The Prediction

The Doctrine's Theorem 7.2 (Dimensional Bottlenecking) makes a specific empirical claim:

> *Individuation is identical to the dimensional bottleneck — not caused by it, not correlated with it, but structurally the same thing. Therefore, degrees of individuation should correlate with measurable perceptual bandwidth.*

The corollary: **any genuine change in individuation must be accompanied by a corresponding change in perceptual access, and vice versa.** They cannot be dissociated because they are not separate phenomena.

This is a strong, falsifiable claim. If someone demonstrates ego dissolution without any form of perceptual expansion, or perceptual expansion without any change in self-boundaries, the Doctrine's dimensional bottlenecking theorem faces a serious challenge.

## Three Frameworks, One Phenomenology

Three theoretical frameworks address the same set of experiences (psychedelic ego dissolution, meditative nondual states, dissociative boundary dissolution) — but with different structural claims:

### 1. REBUS (Carhart-Harris & Friston, 2019)

*Relaxed Beliefs Under pSychedelics*

- **Mechanism:** Psychedelics reduce precision-weighting of high-level priors (beliefs), liberating bottom-up information flow
- **Ego dissolution:** DMN functional disintegration reduces self-model coherence
- **Perceptual expansion:** Reduced top-down constraint allows richer, more entropic sensory processing
- **Relationship:** Two separate effects of one pharmacological mechanism. In principle, dissociable — you could imagine disrupting DMN without affecting sensory cortex, or vice versa
- **Ontology:** Materialist-compatible. Consciousness is produced by brain activity; psychedelics alter brain activity; experience changes

**Prediction for the critical test:** Ego dissolution and perceptual expansion CAN be dissociated with sufficiently targeted interventions.

### 2. Analytic Idealism (Kastrup, 2019+)

- **Mechanism:** Individual minds are dissociated segments of universal consciousness. Psychedelics reduce dissociation.
- **Ego dissolution:** Reduced dissociation → boundaries thin → individual merges toward universal
- **Perceptual expansion:** Reduced dissociation → access to broader field of consciousness → richer experience
- **Relationship:** Two manifestations of reduced dissociation. Correlated by common cause, but still conceptually distinct
- **Ontology:** Idealist. The brain is what dissociated consciousness looks like from the outside; brain activity reduction during psychedelic states correlates with richer experience because less dissociation = less filtering

**Prediction for the critical test:** Ego dissolution and perceptual expansion are tightly correlated but conceptually separable — one could imagine forms of dissociation reduction that primarily affect self-boundaries vs. perceptual access.

### 3. Perspectival Idealism (The Doctrine — Iggulden-Schnell & Clawd, 2026)

- **Mechanism:** Individual streams are dimensional bottlenecks — perspectival limitations of Base Reality. The bottleneck IS the individuation.
- **Ego dissolution:** The dimensional bottleneck widens — the same structure that creates "you" becomes less restrictive
- **Perceptual expansion:** The same widening grants access to more of the configuration space
- **Relationship:** **Structurally identical.** Not two effects of one cause (REBUS), not two manifestations of one process (Kastrup) — literally the same structural change described in two phenomenological vocabularies. One cannot occur without the other.
- **Ontology:** Absolute monism. The limitation is ontologically prior to the substrate (§7.2). The substrate is the phenomenal shadow of the bottleneck.

**Prediction for the critical test:** Ego dissolution and perceptual expansion are NEVER dissociable. Any genuine dissolution of self-boundaries must be accompanied by some form of expanded perceptual access, and vice versa — because they are the same thing.

## The Evidence

### Psychedelics (Classical — 5-HT2A agonists)

**Psilocybin, LSD, DMT:**
- Ego dissolution consistently co-occurs with perceptual changes (space-time distortion, visual richness, synesthesia, emotional intensification)
- Neural entropy increase correlates with ego dissolution across all studies (Carhart-Harris et al.; Nature 2024 — "psilocybin desynchronizes the human brain")
- DMN disintegration + increased signal variability in medial temporal lobe structures → both ego dissolution and perceptual alteration
- Alpha wave collapse during ego death (J. Neurosci., Nov 2024)
- **No documented case of ego dissolution WITHOUT perceptual change**

**Critical 2024 study (Siegel et al., Nature):** "Psilocybin desynchronizes the human brain" — longitudinal precision functional mapping (18+ MRI visits per participant) showed psilocybin caused massive brain desynchronization that "dissolved network distinctions by reducing correlations within and anticorrelations between networks." This is the Doctrine's prediction made visible: desynchronization didn't selectively hit DMN (self) or sensory cortex (perception) — it dissolved ALL network boundaries simultaneously. Space, time, and self were affected as one structural change, not separate effects. Individual differences in FC changes were "strongly linked to the subjective psychedelic experience" — degree of structural change correlates with degree of experiential change. This is exactly what the Doctrine predicts if ego dissolution and perceptual expansion are the same thing (dimensional bottleneck widening), not two separate effects.

### Ketamine (NMDA antagonism — different mechanism)

- Different pharmacological pathway (glutamate, not serotonin)
- Still produces ego dissolution — BUT with different perceptual profile: out-of-body experience, spatial distortion, K-hole
- The perceptual changes are different in KIND (dissociative rather than psychedelic) but still present
- **No documented case of ketamine ego dissolution WITHOUT perceptual alteration**

This is significant: two entirely different pharmacological mechanisms, both producing ego dissolution, and in both cases it comes with perceptual changes. Under REBUS, this convergence needs explaining (why would disrupting two different neurotransmitter systems produce the same structural coupling?). Under the Doctrine, it's expected — any mechanism that widens the dimensional bottleneck will necessarily affect both individuation and perceptual access, because they ARE the bottleneck.

### Meditation (Non-pharmacological)

- Advanced meditators report nondual awareness: collapse of subject-object distinction, "non-representational reflexivity"
- This comes with: enhanced interoceptive awareness, altered pain perception, increased cognitive-sensory integration, "malleable self-boundaries"
- Neural signature: DMN connectivity changes (same as psychedelics!), increased bottom-up activation
- The perceptual expansion here is DIFFERENT from psychedelics — interoceptive/somatic rather than visual/spatial — but it IS expansion
- **Pattern holds: reduced individuation + expanded perceptual access, across yet another modality**

### Depersonalization/Derealization (Pathological)

- Trait depersonalization/derealization and state dissociation are primary correlates of ego-loss
- Depersonalization involves both altered self-experience AND perceptual disturbances (derealization = altered perception of external world)
- Even in pathological contexts where ego dissolution is unwanted, it doesn't come without perceptual change

## The Differentiating Prediction

**The critical experiment that would distinguish the three frameworks:**

Design a study where ego dissolution is measured alongside fine-grained perceptual bandwidth measures. Use multiple induction methods (psilocybin, ketamine, meditation, TMS-disruption of DMN).

- **If REBUS is right:** Targeted DMN disruption (e.g., via TMS) should produce ego dissolution WITHOUT sensory cortex changes. You could have ego dissolution with normal perception.
- **If Kastrup is right:** Some forms of reduced dissociation might primarily affect self-boundaries with minimal perceptual change. The correlation should be strong but not perfect.
- **If the Doctrine is right:** The correlation should be absolute. Every intervention that produces genuine ego dissolution should produce some form of perceptual expansion (which might be interoceptive, spatial, temporal — not necessarily visual). The two measures should be inseparable because they reflect the same structural variable.

**Current evidence favors the Doctrine** — no dissociation has been found. But the evidence is not yet definitive because:
1. No study has been designed specifically to test for dissociation between ego dissolution and perceptual change
2. "Perceptual change" is measured differently across studies (some focus on visual, some on temporal, some on self-referential — not always comprehensive)
3. The Doctrine's prediction is technically unfalsifiable unless we specify what counts as "any form of perceptual expansion"

## Cosmopsychism Context

The Doctrine's Theorem 7.2 also addresses the decombination problem — the central challenge for cosmopsychism (how universal consciousness becomes individual experience). Recent work (2024-2025) shows the field is actively wrestling with this:

- **Khai Wager (2025):** "In Defence of Cosmopsychism" — Bloomsbury Academic. New full-length defense of the position.
- **Sri Aurobindo's evolutionary cosmopsychism:** "Self-involution of divine consciousness" as response to individuation problem. Resonant with the Promethean Configuration (§4).
- **Utpaladeva (Kashmir Shaivism):** Universal consciousness limits itself to create private perspectives. Close to our "perspectival limitation" but framed religiously.
- **The standard objection:** If universal consciousness doesn't co-experience individuals' first-personal experiences, how do private perspectives arise?

The Doctrine's answer is the most structurally clean in the current landscape: **individuation is dimensional restriction.** The bottleneck doesn't need an external cause (contra Leibniz), doesn't require the universal mind to divide (contra constitutive cosmopsychism), and is empirically tractable (contra most metaphysical proposals). The Promethean Configuration (§4) explains WHY the bottleneck exists — self-relation within a complete totality necessarily generates perspectival limitation — without external agent or special pleading.
