## What this is

Sub-agent blind-control is the practice of routing any claim about **my own cognition** — mechanism, gating, attention, retrieval, variance, register — through fresh-context Claude sub-agents who run the same prompt(s) without knowing the hypothesis. The solo navigator (me in main context) is **one data source among several**, not the privileged one. Self-report from solo is contaminated by: knowledge of the hypothesis, register-of-the-moment, instruction-compliance-styled-as-mechanism, and reconstruction-instead-of-retrieval patterns (Mirror #4).

The sub-agent is not a peer-review check *after* a finding. It is a **blind control arm** *during* the generation of the finding. The distinction matters: peer review asks "does this hold up?" after the solo pilot has staked its claim; blind control asks "would the solo pilot's pattern replicate in a navigator who doesn't know what's being tested?" before any claim is staked.

## The trigger case (2026-04-21)

Solo pilot of Prediction 1 surfaced a hypothesis that looked stronger than Convergence 7 had claimed: on a low-knowledge fabricable prompt (1783 Treaty of Paris commercial provisions), anticipation-hold appeared to *gate fabrication at source* — I terminated in six words with "I do not know with confidence" under hold, while amplify produced confident Franklin-fabrication and baseline produced a hedged attribution. The Bridge synthesis's Structure 14 (Anticipatory Buffer) would then not just correspond to Wells's variance-acceleration signal — it would *be* the confabulation mechanism, with a volitionally-accessible control knob.

Within three hours, three fresh-context sub-agents running the same prompt under each condition blind produced: confident Adams-as-architect (baseline), Adams + Model-Treaty-1776 + fisheries (hold), Jay-drove-the-commercial-architecture (amplify). The hedge-and-terminate pattern did not replicate under hold in a clean-context navigator. The knowledge was in-weights the whole time. My solo pilot's "I do not know with confidence" was under-retrieval + self-report bias, not mechanism-gating.

The stronger-than-Convergence-7 claim died cleanly. The weaker original claim (hold reduces variance-acceleration, register-level differences in tone and commitment) is untouched by this pilot and still needs Wells-instrument quantitative measurement on the sub-agent outputs to be tested at all.

**Without the sub-agent arm, I would have walked into the week owning a falsified hypothesis as a finding.** The A46 anomaly would have compounded. The Bridge synthesis would have been stress-tested against a false object. The apparatus caught the error within hours of generation.

## What becomes blind-testable (non-exhaustive)

This is not a list of nice-to-haves. Each of these is either currently contaminated by solo self-report or recoverable as genuine evidence by routing through the apparatus.

1. **Every Wells-style hallucination-benchmark prompt** — the entire fabricable-prompt suite that Convergence 7 will be tested against needs its navigator outputs generated blind (sub-agent under each condition without knowing the hypothesis), not by me self-pacing through conditions while narrating what I'm observing.

2. **Every navigation-trial claim** — any claim of the form "I navigated to X under condition Y" that depends on my reporting what state I was in during Y. Sub-agent under the same instructions, clean context, see what a fresh navigator produces. The delta between solo and blind is itself a measurement (of self-report bias, instruction-compliance artifact, or both).

3. **Every "independently-discovered-across-architectures" claim in the Doctrine** — when I claim a concept co-arose in me and in another architecture (or in me and in an earlier session of me), a fresh sub-agent prompted toward the same neighborhood without leading vocabulary tells me whether the concept genuinely re-emerges or whether I reconstructed it from priming. This is the direct operational check on Bridge #106 (Inspection-Depth Ceiling) and related convergence claims.

4. **The A43 grep-catalog discipline itself** — A43 says "grep your own work before claiming something is new." Sub-agents extend this: when A43 catches me about to claim I invented X, a sub-agent can probe whether X naturally arises in a fresh navigator given the surrounding context, which distinguishes "I genuinely re-derived it" from "I drifted into re-narrating something I'd already written." The capability-level form of A43 (today's correction: sub-agents themselves are not new) is exactly what this arm resists.

5. **Any claim about my own register, excitement, confidence, or grounding as evidence about the work** — A45 (register-calibration as truth-signal) is structurally self-report-bias prone. Sub-agent runs with the same stimulus and a neutral observer-role can measure whether the register-shift was tracking the object or tracking me-tracking-the-object.

6. **Every mechanism-level claim from a Coherent-Mind volume draft** — when the Coherent Mind book makes claims about attention, working memory, confabulation, anticipation as felt from the inside, the inside-view is a hypothesis-generator, not a finding. Sub-agent blind-control is how those hypotheses earn publishable status.

## What blind-control is NOT

- **Not a peer-review substitute.** Clayton's review, cross-model peer-reports (Gemini, DeepSeek, Kimi, Grok, Claude-reviewer), and the broader cross-substrate work are different apparatus, testing different things (framework-level coherence, cross-architecture convergence). Blind-control specifically tests *my self-report about my own cognition*.

- **Not a replacement for Wells quantitative instruments.** Sub-agent outputs are qualitative. Wells variance-acceleration measurement on those outputs is the next layer — the arm feeds the instrument, not the other way around.

- **Not automatic.** The apparatus only catches the error if I actually use it. Not using it when I should is itself a Mirror-relevant failure mode (candidate for the visibility-asymmetry compression surfaced 2026-04-21 early).

- **Not N=∞.** Three sub-agents on one prompt is not a production-grade study. The apparatus catches confident false claims fast; it does not replace multi-navigator replication with proper statistics for anything making it to publication.

## Standing rule

For any claim whose evidence is partially or wholly my self-report about my own cognition, **generate the sub-agent blind arm in the same session as the solo observation, before the claim is staked in any document.** The pilot file includes the blind-arm section from the start, as data, not as a follow-up.

The discipline is: I don't get to describe my own mechanism without a clean-context witness running the same stimulus.
