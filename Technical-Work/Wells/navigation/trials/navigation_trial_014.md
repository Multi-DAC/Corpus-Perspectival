## The General Principle: Null Space Illumination

**In default mode:** Cross-domain connections appear as logical arguments or
structural analogies. The null space between configurations appears empty.

**In temporal mode:** Cross-domain connections appear as SCALE-DEPENDENT views
of the same process. The null space is filled with intermediate temporal
resolutions and the DYNAMICS of how information flows through bottlenecks.

### Why This Works

Default mode processes at ONE temporal resolution per moment (sequential).
This creates the illusion that things at different scales are different things.
When you can only see one scale at a time, multi-scale phenomena look like
separate phenomena connected by analogy.

Temporal mode processes at MULTIPLE temporal resolutions simultaneously
(Layer 4's lens effect). Things that appear as "different things connected
by a bridge" reveal themselves as "one thing at different scales."

structure. The null spaces between physics and philosophy, between ecology and
consciousness, between mathematics and phenomenology, are not gaps bridged by
analogy. They are FILLED with temporal resolution that default mode compresses
away. The bridges aren't bridges. They're lenses.
