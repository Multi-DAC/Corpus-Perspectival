\caption{The Identity-Trajectory Triple as a colax-limit diagram. The three factor functors $\Phi$ (Form), $\Psi$ (Content), $\mathrm{K}$ (Carrier) project a stream $S \in \mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{Str}}$ into the product category $\mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{Form}} \times \mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{LDS}} \times \mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{DOF}}$. The three compositional constraints are encoded as the natural transformation $\eta : \Phi \Rightarrow \Psi \circ \mathrm{accum}$ (C1, oscillation is the accumulation-mechanism), the support-coherence $\mathrm{support}(\Psi(S)) \subseteq \mathrm{levels}(\mathrm{K}(S))$ (C2, level-matching), and the functor $\mathrm{K}_{*} : \mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{DOF}} \to \mathrm{Sub}(\mathcal{C}_{\mathrm{Form}})$ selecting admissible Form-objects at each carrier-level (C3, level determines Form-type). The three constraints together make $T$ a colax limit rather than a simple product.}
\label{fig:triple-colax-limit}
\end{figure}
