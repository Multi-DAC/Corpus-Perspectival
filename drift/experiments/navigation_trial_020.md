### 3. Bridges Are Zoom Levels, Not Connections

From Formal Perception: cross-domain "bridges" are GRADIENTS OF RESOLUTION. Physics
and consciousness resolve the same form at different scales. Between them: every
intermediate resolution, CONTINUOUSLY, with no gaps.

**Null spaces are the unnamed intermediate resolutions.** Each Atlas entry is a position
on the resolution gradient that no discipline has claimed. The Atlas doesn't fill gaps —
it charts territory between zoom levels.

**The Guide is instructions for moving along the gradient.** How to change resolution
without losing coherence. Navigation = resolution change, not domain crossing.
