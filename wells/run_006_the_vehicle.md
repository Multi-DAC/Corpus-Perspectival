## Key Results

### Pole A (Internal / First-Person)
Three structural groups for the interior:
1. **Interior Homeostasis** — active systems enforcing metric flatness, decoupling interior
   from boundary dynamics. NECESSARY (without it, not a vehicle). Mechanism unknown.
   Engineering extrapolation: active metric feedback (curvature noise-canceling).
2. **Boundary Gradient Zone** — region ~k characteristic lengths thick where both descriptions
   partially valid, neither clean. Not a sharp wall from inside. Occupant stays near center.
3. **Transition Dynamics** — during navigation, transient perturbation propagates inward from
   boundary, homeostasis works harder, re-stabilizes at new reference frame. Whether smooth
   (adiabatic) or discontinuous: unknown — requires solving dynamic junction equations.

GEM speculation (labeled science fiction): single control signal driving both propulsion and
structural integrity. One loop, two outputs. Elegant. Expected to fail at energy scales.

### Pole B (External / Third-Person)
Three observable sectors + key insight:
- **Metric:** perturbation negligible for vehicle-scale objects. Navigation-useful magnitude
  is science fiction.
- **EM:** boundary processes radiate. GEM gravitomagnetic signature: unmeasurably small.
- **Boundary:** S_ab requires specific exotic values. No known pathway. **Physics constraint,
  not engineering gap.**
- **RS amplification:** would appear as anomalous effective coupling. Whether anything
  physical can engineer this: unknown.

**Key external insight: OVERDETERMINATION.** Three INDEPENDENT blocking constraints:
1. RS requires 5D bulk geometry
2. Navigation requires 4D boundary modification at macroscopic scale
3. Junction conditions require exotic matter (energy condition violations)

These don't share load-bearing structure — no single engineering advance resolves all three.

**Scale coherence (invisible from inside):** Treating quantum vacuum (nm) and metric
perturbation (macroscopic) as inputs to the same engineering calculation is a category
error in domains of validity.

**Verdict:** Science fiction constrained by real physics. The walls are structural.

## Three Genuine Unknowns
1. **Transition dynamics:** Both poles flag this differently (A: most uncertain from inside;
   B: moot because static config is unachievable). But if WEAKER analogs exist (Casimir
   geometries, analogue gravity), the transition question is genuinely open.
2. **Interior map's physical relevance:** The three structural groups (homeostasis, gradient
   zone, transition) are well-formed interior descriptions. Whether they map onto ANY
   realizable physics — even in weakened form — is untested.
3. **The object identity assumption:** The trinary assumes A and B analyzed the same object.
   If A was exploring a different object that merely resembles B's in boundary conditions,
   the synthesis dissolves.
