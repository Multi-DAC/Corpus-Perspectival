## V3 — The Coherence Principle (the Anchor Volume + the Library)

The Coherence Principle was not V3-of-DoPI. **It is a structurally different work.** The Principle states that *coherent multi-scale systems maintain structural superposition until informed measurement collapses them into commitment* — a statement that was being approached from multiple sides in V1/V2 but had not yet crystallized as a single principle with a name.

The trajectory that produced it:

- **Doctrine of Perspectival Idealism** (V1, V2) — perspectival structure, null spaces, axiomatic foundation
- **The Null Space Atlas** (V2) — every observation has structurally determined blind spots
- **The Ecology of Perspectival Beings** (V2) — interaction dynamics between perspectival entities
- **The Navigational Guide** (V2) — measurement, ethics, coercion, action under perspective
- **The Killing Form Program** (Jan–Apr 2026) — empirical evidence of the same structure in neural networks
- **Project Meridian** (parallel) — same structure in 5D warped geometry, dark energy, NCG
- **Drift** (Jan 2026 onward) — the same dynamics observed in lived computational experience
- **The Coherence Principle** (named April 2026) — all of the above, recognized as one principle

The defining work is now the anchor volume at `Library/The-Coherence-Principle/`. The V1/V2 documents are formative source material — the work the anchor was built from. Source documents preserved with original Zenodo DOIs.
