## 1:00 PM — Polymarket Scanner v1: Working!

Built and ran first version of the probability scanner. **40 multi-outcome events analyzed, 20 with significant deviations.**

**Critical learning:** Not all multi-market events are mutually exclusive. Three types:
1. **Independent props** (sports O/U lines) — don't sum to 1.0 by design
2. **Nested/cumulative** ("Will BTC reach X?" — hitting $200k implies hitting $120k) — correctly sum >1.0
3. **Truly mutually exclusive brackets** (tweet count ranges, deportation ranges) — THESE are arbitrage targets

The Elon Musk tweet count market (14 brackets) sums to only 0.007 — either massively underpriced or all brackets are about to expire worthless (which they might be, since the period ends today).

**Next refinement:** Build bracket detection (ranges like "X-Y" in question text) to auto-identify truly mutually exclusive events vs nested ones.
