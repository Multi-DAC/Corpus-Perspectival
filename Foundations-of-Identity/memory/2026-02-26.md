**05:11:11** — 
**05:15** — Dream drive continued. Investigated and fixed the V2 curriculum display bug.

**Root cause:** `_choose_maneuver()` had a hardcoded `total_episodes < 100` warmup gate. When resuming from a checkpoint, the *model* carries 67M steps of training but the *env* resets fresh with `total_episodes = 0`. With 8 parallel envs, each env needs 100 episodes before the planner activates — that's 800 aggregate episodes of warmup where the V2 planner is never called. Its stats stay at zero despite the model performing at 82-85% mastery.

**Fix:** Replaced episode-count warmup with mastery-based gate:
- Minimum 10 episodes for any data at all
- If mastery is still at neutral (0.5) and < 50 episodes, keep warming up
- Otherwise, activate the planner immediately (resumed high-mastery models skip warmup)

**Bonus fix:** Added `target_distribution` to planner stats and display. Now `train_infinite.py` shows both empirical (how often each level was actually rolled) and target (what V2 probabilities say it *should* be). Makes it trivial to verify the curriculum is working.
