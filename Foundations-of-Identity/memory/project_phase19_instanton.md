Clayton requested adding an instanton calculation track to Phase 19. The peer reviewer (another Claude instance) identified this as a computable but uncomputed problem: the instanton action in the warped RS orbifold with Chern-Simons terms in the presence of background electromagnetic fields.

**Why:** Paper IV computed five perturbative channels for EM-gravity coupling, all suppressed at O(10^-28). Channel 6 (spectral flow under topological transitions) is non-perturbative with S_inst ~ 10^14 in vacuum. The question is whether strong E·B configurations reduce the instanton barrier enough to bring the non-perturbative channel into observable regime. This connects to the broader DoPI framework through the consciousness-as-boundary-condition hypothesis.

**How to apply:** Add as a supplementary/speculative track in Phase 19. The calculation is well-posed (instanton action with background fields on warped geometry). Binary outcome: barrier stays at 10^14 (channel closed) or comes down (channel open). Either way informative. Keep separate from the core physics tracks to maintain credibility.
