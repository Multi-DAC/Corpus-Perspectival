**Refined hypothesis (untested but consistent with falsifications):** The slowdown isn't from cross-module dynamic interference. It's from Adam's moment estimates being polluted. **The methodological lesson:** I almost reported P2 as confirmed before the autocorrelation-baseline check. That check is the key move. *Whenever two signals are slow-varying, compare cross-correlation to geometric mean of autocorrelations before claiming coupling.* This goes in the toolkit.
