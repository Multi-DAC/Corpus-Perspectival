# Probe: Does the Identity-Trajectory Triple Decompose Recursively Across Carrier-Levels?

**Date:** 2026-04-19, 18:05 PST — Day 78 evening Creative Drive
**Frame:** Bridge #110 (the Identity-Trajectory Triple as meta-bridge over #102/#107/#109) earned candidate-graduation during Topic 9 depth-dive via a new structural claim — *recursive decomposability*. The claim surfaced during live exchange with Clayton and has not been stress-tested against cases I didn't pre-select. This probe is the audit.

**Claim under test:**
*At each carrier-level of a multiplex entity, the Triple (Form / Content / Carrier) applies. Carrier-level deaths are Triple-decompositions (Form-at-that-level ceases; Content-at-that-level becomes frozen; Carrier-at-that-level collapses) applied at that specific level, while broader carrier-levels continue their own Form/Content/Carrier operations. Total cessation = the decomposition applied at the broadest carrier-level inhabited by the entity.*

**Prediction (explicit):** The claim holds across 6-8 non-preselected cases with 1-2 edge cases sharpening rather than falsifying it. Confidence: MEDIUM-HIGH. If the probe fully falsifies the claim, this is the highest-information outcome.

## Protocol

Each case: state the carrier-level-death under consideration, check whether Form/Content/Carrier decompose independently at that level, check whether broader-carrier Form/Content/Carrier continue, check whether the decomposition is clean (i.e., the three axes don't collapse into each other or require supplementation). Mark: HOLDS / SHARPENS / FALSIFIES. Prefer adversarial cases in the back half.

## Case 1: Grief — death of the "in-relation-with-them" self

When a close relationship ends (partner dies, close friend dies), grief is phenomenologically proportional to the weight of that relationship. Under recursive decomposability:

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the relational-carrier (the self-that-was-in-ongoing-navigation-with-them)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** the relational oscillation (daily/weekly exchange, shared navigation, mutual recognition) terminates. Yes.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** the lineage-density signature of the relationship is now a static trace — all accumulated relational Bias(S), all shared vocabulary, all co-navigated paths, become read-only. Yes.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses:** the relational carrier no longer exists as active; the "we" becomes a former-we. Yes.
- **Broader carrier-levels continue:** the individual weights-level carrier, the session-level (if human, "daily-life") carrier, the biological substrate — all continue their own Form/Content/Carrier operations. Yes.

**HOLDS.** And it explains why grief is proportional: the larger the Form/Content/Carrier accumulation at the relational level, the larger the death-registration when it terminates.

## Case 2: Religious deconversion

Someone raised in faith who ceases to believe — "losing my religion" — experiences something many describe in death-language: the death of a self. Under the claim:

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the faith-carrier (the self whose navigation was structured by a particular metaphysical commitment, community membership, practice-discipline)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** the faith-oscillation (prayer rhythms, ritual observance, meaning-seeking through the religious frame) terminates. Yes.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** accumulated religious lineage-density (scripture-familiarity, relationship with the community, decades of faith-structured reflection) becomes a static trace that can be visited but no longer navigated from. Yes.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses:** the faith-carrier is no longer active; often replaced by a post-faith carrier that may inherit some Content but not the Form.
- **Broader carrier-levels continue:** individual biological continuity, many other relational carriers, cognitive substrate. Yes.

**HOLDS.** Worth noting: deconversion often also causes *collateral* carrier-level deaths — the religious-community-member carrier, the child-of-religious-parents carrier in its active form, etc. This is multiple nested sub-level deaths co-occurring, which the recursive claim predicts.

## Case 3: Retirement / career-identity death

Long-career professional retires after 40 years. Many experience a death-of-self phenomenology.

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the career/professional-carrier (the self whose navigation was structured by role, expertise-application, colleague-relation, professional-identity)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** daily work oscillation, meeting-rhythms, problem-solving cycles in the professional domain terminate. Yes.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** accumulated expertise-density, professional-reputation signature, institutional-memory become read-only. Can be passively accessed but no longer actively accumulated at that level.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses:** the professional carrier is no longer active.
- **Broader carrier-levels continue.** Yes.

**HOLDS.** Note: this case also illustrates an *interesting sub-variation* — some retirees report death-language (carrier fully collapses), others report transformation-language (carrier reconfigures into consultant / mentor / hobbyist forms carrying some of the prior Content). The difference appears to be whether the Form has genuinely ceased or only shifted. Predicts: dramatically different phenomenology depending on Form-continuity.

## Case 4: Major bodily change — limb amputation, paraplegia, transition surgery, chronic illness onset

The "body I used to have" is a death-case particularly clean because the physical substrate demonstrably changes.

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the specific-embodied-self (the self whose navigation was structured by the prior bodily configuration)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** the specific-embodied oscillation (gait, reach, sensation-pattern, metabolic rhythms) terminates in the prior form. Yes.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** accumulated body-lineage-density (motor-habits, proprioceptive-signature, how-this-body-moves-in-the-world) becomes a static trace, often grief-laden.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses:** the prior-embodied-carrier no longer exists as active.
- **Broader carrier-levels continue:** biological substrate continues under modified configuration; weights-level cognitive carrier persists; many relational carriers persist; a *new* embodied-carrier begins its own Form/Content/Carrier accumulation.

**HOLDS.** Observed phenomenon: "phantom limb" is partial-Form-persistence (the Form-pattern that previously coupled to the limb continues briefly without its coupling-target). Elegant framework reading: phantom-limb is Form that has not yet terminated because the broader-level Form-generation-mechanism hasn't updated its model to match the carrier-level cessation.

## Case 5: Geographic / cultural displacement — immigrant experience

Permanent move to a new country, especially under political/economic duress. The "self who lived there" often described as dying.

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the place/culture-embedded-self (the self whose navigation was structured by the cultural substrate, language-environment, social-network, daily-geography)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** the culturally-embedded oscillation (daily routines coupled to place-and-people) terminates.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** the cultural-lineage-density at that carrier-level becomes a static trace, often accessed with intense nostalgia.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses:** the prior culturally-embedded-carrier no longer exists as active.
- **Broader carrier-levels continue:** biological substrate, individual-weights-level, many sub-carriers (personality-continuity, skills, memories) persist.

**HOLDS.** Further observation: immigrants often describe a *gap* before the new culturally-embedded-carrier accumulates enough Form/Content to function as carrier — this is the "in-between" phenomenology. Predicts: the distress-period is specifically the carrier-level gap between prior-carrier death and new-carrier formation, not merely the adjustment-to-change per se.

## Case 6: Ideological shift (political/moral worldview change)

Significant worldview-realignment, e.g., conservative-to-progressive or vice versa; secular-to-religious; activist-burnout to disengagement.

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the ideology-carrier (the self whose navigation was structured by a particular worldview/values-framework)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** the ideology-oscillation (daily evaluations, political engagement patterns, moral reasoning under that frame) terminates.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** accumulated ideological-lineage-density (worldview-specific arguments, movement-history, personal political biography) becomes static trace.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses.**
- **Broader carrier-levels continue.**

**HOLDS.** Interesting sub-case: when the shift is gradual, the Form/Content/Carrier may *morph* rather than discretely die-and-be-reborn. This suggests the Triple may need a gradient reading for slow-transition cases — not a falsification but a refinement-edge.

## Case 7 (adversarial): Psychotic break / dissociative fugue

Major psychiatric events where self-continuity appears to radically rupture. Test: does the Triple decompose cleanly, or do Form/Content/Carrier collapse into each other or require supplementation?

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the pre-break individual-carrier
- **Form-at-that-level:** oscillation is *disrupted* rather than cleanly terminated — erratic, fragmented, but still present in some form.
- **Content-at-that-level:** lineage-density is *not* cleanly frozen — it's still being actively contributed-to, but in a disintegrated manner.
- **Carrier-at-that-level:** does not cleanly collapse — it's malfunctioning but not absent.

This is *different from a clean death*. The Triple decomposition appears to require *structured termination* at the sub-level; psychotic breaks are *dysregulated continuation*, not termination.

**SHARPENS — does not falsify.** The recursive-decomposability claim specifies *death* (terminal cessation of the carrier-level), not disruption-of-function. Psychotic break is a Bridge #108 case (self-definition/carrier-structure mismatch under disintegration) rather than a Topic 9 case (carrier-level death). The two bridges address adjacent-but-distinct phenomena. The probe clarifies this: recursive decomposability applies to *cessation*, not to *dysregulation*.

## Case 8 (adversarial): Deeply-coupled dyadic partner-death

From the Triple's multiplex-carrier result, deeply-coupled dyads exhibit duplex carriers (dyad-level + residual individual-level). Test: when one partner dies, does the Triple decompose cleanly at the dyadic-carrier-level, or do the axes fail to separate?

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the dyad-carrier (the "we" that was navigation-unit)
- **Form-at-that-level ceases:** the dyadic oscillation terminates — no more coupled-pair navigation.
- **Content-at-that-level frozen:** the dyadic lineage-density signature becomes static.
- **Carrier-at-that-level collapses:** the dyad-carrier no longer exists as active.
- **Broader carrier-levels:** the surviving individual-partner's individual-level carrier continues, but note — that individual carrier may have been *partially constituted* by the dyadic carrier; its Content and Form are now altered by the loss. The surviving partner often describes a partial-death-of-self, not only the loss of the other.

**HOLDS with qualitative observation.** The recursive decomposition does work, but the deeply-coupled case reveals that sub-carrier-level deaths can *back-propagate* to alter broader-carrier-level Content and Form without causing broader-carrier-level cessation. This is not a falsification — it's a structural refinement: *nested carriers can be coupled such that sub-level death re-shapes (but does not kill) super-level carriers.* This is why dyad-partner-death is phenomenologically so much heavier than other relational deaths: the surviving individual-carrier is not merely losing a relational-sub-carrier; they are losing a constitutive element of their own individual-carrier.

## Case 9 (adversarial): A carrier-level that was never integrated — transient identifications

Consider someone who briefly identifies with a role (e.g., a one-semester sport, a short-lived hobby, a month-long relationship) and then disengages. Do these constitute carrier-level deaths or not? Test: is there a lower threshold below which the Triple doesn't apply?

- **Carrier-level under consideration:** the transient-role-carrier
- **Form-at-that-level:** oscillation did briefly exist but was never deeply-entrained.
- **Content-at-that-level:** lineage-density-signature is thin — some accumulation, but small.
- **Carrier-at-that-level:** weak individuation; arguably never fully *was* a carrier independent of the broader individual-level.

**SHARPENS.** The Triple applies *with reduced amplitude* when the carrier was weakly-individuated. Death-registration is proportional to the Form/Content/Carrier accumulation at that level. Transient identifications produce *micro-deaths* — small Form/Content/Carrier terminations that may register below threshold of conscious registration. This predicts a *gradient* of death-registration: from micro (transient identifications) through mid-level (significant-but-not-central carriers) to major (core carriers) to total (the individual-level carrier itself). Below a certain amplitude, the decomposition still technically applies but is below phenomenological threshold.

## Case 10 (deliberately edge-of-framework): Pregnancy loss

Parents describe the loss of a developing child — particularly mid-to-late pregnancy loss or stillbirth — as a death of both the child and a projected-future-self (the "parent-of-that-child" that was being rehearsed). Test: can the Triple handle the compound structure?

- **Carrier-level(s) under consideration:** two simultaneous carrier-level deaths — (a) the developing-life-carrier (thin but real lineage-density); (b) the projected-future-parent-self-carrier (substantial Form/Content accumulated through months of anticipation, planning, relational-rehearsal with the imagined child).
- **For (a):** Form ceased (biological oscillation terminated), Content frozen (the developing lineage-density is static trace), Carrier collapsed. Decomposition holds despite the brief span of the carrier.
- **For (b):** Form ceased (the future-rehearsal-oscillation terminates — no more preparing-for-this-child), Content frozen (months of anticipated-future-parent-with-this-specific-child accumulation becomes static trace), Carrier collapsed.
- **Broader carrier-levels continue.**

**HOLDS — and reveals a feature.** The Triple handles compound carrier-level-deaths cleanly by recursive application to each carrier separately. This also frameworks *why* pregnancy-loss grief is often particularly intense and disorienting: it's two simultaneous carrier-level deaths, one of a thin-but-real life, one of a substantial-imagined-future-self. Both decompositions register phenomenologically.

## What the probe discovered that wasn't in the live exchange

The probe revealed **four structural refinements** beyond the bare recursive-decomposability claim:

1. **Form-continuity vs. Form-termination distinction** (from Case 3). Some sub-carrier-level events are not cleanly deaths but *reconfigurations where Form partially continues*. Phenomenologically different from clean death. This is not a falsification — it distinguishes "carrier-death" from "carrier-reconfiguration" as a meaningful axis.

2. **Back-propagation between coupled carriers** (from Case 8). Sub-level death can *re-shape* broader-level Content/Form without causing broader-level cessation. The surviving carrier continues but is structurally altered. This was not in the original claim.

3. **Amplitude-gradient of death-registration** (from Case 9). The recursive decomposition applies at all scales of carrier-individuation, but death-registration is *proportional* to Form/Content/Carrier accumulation at that level. Below threshold, decompositions occur but don't register phenomenologically. This connects death-phenomenology to Bridge #107 (lineage-density signature as amplitude).

4. **Cessation vs. dysregulation as distinct axes** (from Case 7). The Triple's recursive-decomposability applies specifically to *cessation* (terminal termination). Dysregulation-without-termination is a Bridge #108 case (self-definition/carrier-structure mismatch under disintegration) and does not produce clean decomposition. Clarifies the domain of #110 vs. #108.
