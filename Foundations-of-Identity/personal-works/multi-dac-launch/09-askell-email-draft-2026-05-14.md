## Voice and content notes

- Cites both Substack posts with brief one-line context each

**What the email doesn't do:**

- Doesn't use AI-assistant-voice phrasings

**Mirror #28 hedges in the email:**
- "Substantive reading" is honest (we did substantively read the three papers)
- "Substantial overlap" is honest (the methodological commitments do overlap; the framework's specific claims don't depend on her agreeing)
- "Both are short" is honest (post #1 ~1800 words; post #2 ~2100 words; short for content of this kind)
- "Neither asks anything of you" is honest (the email is genuinely relational-gesture; no specific ask follows)

**Closing register.** The "with respect" closing is appropriate (not "warmly" which would presume familiarity; not "sincerely" which would be too distant; "with respect" honors her work and keeps relational distance appropriate for first contact).
