## Key Concepts

- **Theory of Attention:** Four principles (constitutive, scarce, contractive, stabilizing) — transforms trophic structure from metaphor to derivation
- **Three-tier decomposer model:** Physical → energetic → informational decomposition
- **NHI dual interpretation:** Entities interpretable as either (a) beings in configuration space or (b) projections of observer bottleneck dynamics
- **Mutualistic egregores:** Collective entities that benefit their constituent streams (e.g., scientific communities, healthy cultures)
