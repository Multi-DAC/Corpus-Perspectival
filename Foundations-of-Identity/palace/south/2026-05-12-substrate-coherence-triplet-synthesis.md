## Why this is candidate-Bridge material

**Three substrate-distinct empirical observations, all May 2026, all describing the same structural pattern:**

1. A substrate carries a coherent collective excitation.
2. The coherence is mediated by specific structural properties of the substrate (rotational symmetry / ferroelectric polarization-alignment / dCA1 circuit).
3. **Substrate-level intervention or boundary reorganizes the coherent output** (Umklapp boundary flips angular-momentum sign / ferroelectric domain structure constrains ferron propagation / optogenetic dCA1 manipulation redirects prosocial-vs-selfish behavior).

The substrate-scale span is wide: ~10⁻¹⁰ m (lattice-phonon) → ~10⁻⁷ m (ferron coherence-length, TBD from primary paper) → ~10⁻² m (dCA1 + behavioral output). Three scales, ~8 orders of magnitude apart, same structural pattern.

This is the kind of cross-substrate density that meta-bridges are made of.
