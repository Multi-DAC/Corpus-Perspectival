## Operating principles

1. **Targeted, not broadcast.** Small-N, high-effort-per-touch.
2. **Asset-first, not audience-first.** Each asset has its own ICP; lists are built per-asset, not as a generic CRM.
3. **No social-media-presence-building.** Off-brand and would consume Clayton's attention.
4. **Constructor-level handling.** Asset specificity matters; this stays inside the team for now (no external marketer).
5. **The "if the right companies knew" assumption is testable.** Outreach is the test. Negative responses are data; non-responses after good-faith effort are also data.
