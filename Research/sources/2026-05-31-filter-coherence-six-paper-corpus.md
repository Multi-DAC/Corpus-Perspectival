## A. The consciousness quintet (→ Monday)

**A1. Williams, G. (2023). "The Brain as a Filter: Introducing a Quantum Ground into IIT" (FIIT).** Qeios, doi 10.32388/5ZA5SZ.
Russellian-monist supplement to IIT: structure can't ground phenomenal experience → needs an intrinsic element (quantum ground) → cosmopsychism; brain as filter/tuner, not generator. *Framework hook:* filter-not-generator ≅ our carrier-not-generator (M14); Russellian inscrutables ≅ "structure needs an intrinsic ground"; §10 "filtering" + ch1 perspective-as-localized-activation are the formal version. *Honest cut:* his "lights out for any computer" smuggles a carbon-restriction into a substrate-neutral monism; A1.4 rejects it.

**A2. Lomas, Masters, Brown & Wheeler (2026). "Celestial (Be)Longing."** *Philosophy and Cosmology* 36, 4–101.
98-pp survey of the ACE/NHI hypothesis; ends (Sacraterrestrial → Metaterrestrial) on idealism / panpsychism / consciousness-as-field / simulation → "back to God." *Hook:* arrives at consciousness-as-substrate from the UAP direction (convergence signature, M15-like); filter/antenna model recurs; Promethean through-line ↔ Universal-Coherence; "AI-as-NHI" brackets the substrate-independence question. *Discipline flag:* podcast/whistleblower-heavy; phenomenology survey, not evidence (Master Glossary §11 discrimination applies).

**A3. Evans, J. "Encounters with Negative Entities."** ecstaticintegration.org.
Survey of malevolent-entity encounters under psychedelics (ayahuasca 46% / DMT 36% / LSD 17% / psilocybin 12%; JHU DMT survey 11% "demons"); three frames — Jungian-shadow / shamanic-independent / pragmatic-psychiatric. *Hook:* the three frames = three measurement stances on one felt phenomenon; Coherent-Mind feed; Monday phenomenology texture.

**A4. Ruban, A. & van Elk, M. (2025). "Psychedelics as Non-Specific Amplifiers."** *J. Anomalous Experience & Cognition* 5(2), 212–231, doi 10.31156/jaex.27866.
The skeptic: psychedelics amplify pre-existing beliefs within cultural feedback loops; don't reveal unmediated truth. Timmermann metaphysical-belief shifts small + directional-preserving; SEBUS/REBUS/ALBUS; "politically pluripotent"; caution with metaphysics; calls for a *relational ontology* (Barad). *Hook:* the central impasse it can't settle (filter loosening = "see reality as-is" vs "amplify the set") is resolved by A1.3 (navigation-not-generation) + A1.2 (experience-as-projection); Barad's relational ontology is what the Coherence framework formalizes.

**A5. Michael, Luke & Robinson (2025). "An encounter with death: DMT vs NDE."** *Front. Psychol.* 16:1532937.
DMT reproduces NDE *structure* (template) but diverges in *content* (texture) — "NDE-mimetic," not "NDE-ogen." Neurophenomenology (Varela/Petitmengin): structure = invariant/bottom-up/universal; content = variable/top-down/set-shaped. Convergent downstream mechanism (5HT2A / NMDA / kappa-opioid all → DMN disruption). *Hook (strongest of the five):* structure/content ≅ F₁/F₂ parallel projection (A1.2) + Form/Content orthogonal-but-constrained (TC1), independently derived + empirically operationalized; it *resolves* A4-vs-A1 empirically (filter-view right about structure, amplifier-view right about content); convergent mechanism = M15 + substrate-invariance. DMN-as-filter spine runs through A1, A4, A5.

## B. Coupling-as-coherence (→ Tuesday)

**B1. Van der Weel & Van der Meer (2024). "Handwriting but not typewriting leads to widespread brain connectivity."** *Front. Psychol.* 14:1219945.
256-ch EEG: handwriting → widespread theta/alpha connectivity across parietal–central hubs (memory/learning-relevant); typing → not. Driver = rich multi-modal coupling (vision ⊥ motor ⊥ proprioception) bound across regions; typing collapses orthogonal axes to one keypress (same output, no binding, no learning). *Hook:* biological existence-proof of Coherence Principle **Condition 1** (orthogonal DOF + ι⊣κ binding) and of "coherence can't be bolted on." Maps tier-3-consolidation:tier-2-retrieval :: handwriting:typing. Theta band ≈ Phase-1 EM platform 4 Hz probe.
