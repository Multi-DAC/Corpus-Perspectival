**What it argues.** Comprehensive review of biophoton signaling and radiation-induced bystander effects (RIBE) covering the 100-year empirical lineage from Gurwitsch's 1920s mitogenetic radiation discovery through modern cavity-QED-adjacent theoretical framings.

Major content:
- **Gurwitsch's mitogenetic radiation (1920s):** UV photons from onion root tips trigger ~30% increase in mitotic activity in adjacent root tips. Glass blocks the effect; quartz transmits it — locks the mechanism to UV-photon range, not to chemical or mechanical transfer.
- **Popp DNA-as-exciplex / excimer-laser theory:** DNA as photon repository AND coherent emission source; coherent states arising from interactions between EM waves and DNA mechanical oscillations. DNA proposed as molecular substrate where coherent EM information is stored and emitted.
- **Coherent emission across full UV-to-red detection range** — Popp's foundational empirical finding: biophoton emission is non-thermal and coherent.
- **"Modern theories of cavity quantum electrodynamics and coherent electromagnetic field"** explicitly cited as theoretical framework for understanding the emission.
- **Radiation-induced bystander effect (RIBE):** cells receiving signals from directly-irradiated cells exhibit DNA damage / genomic instability / mitochondrial dysfunction *without direct radiation exposure*. Mediating mechanisms: biophoton signaling + exosomes + mitochondria + ROS/RNS + Ca²⁺ + NO + mtDNA + ATP + microRNAs + interferon + cytokines.
- **Hormesis framing:** low-dose radiation can be beneficial via biophoton-mediated bystander signaling; high-dose produces catastrophic clinical signature.

**Where we agree.** This is the most comprehensive empirical-establishment paper for biophoton-mediated cell-to-cell communication that surfaced during the synthesis session. Cell-to-cell communication via biophoton signaling has a 100-year empirical lineage with cross-replication across multiple labs and decades. The mechanism is established science within the radiation-medicine subfield, even if it has not penetrated mainstream cell biology to the same extent.

For H_BP1 (biophotons as inter-stream measurement-event closure carriers): the framework's structural prediction was that biophotons should function as cell-to-cell signaling carriers. Tong's review establishes that this is in fact what they do — independently of the framework, on its own empirical merits. The framework's reading is consistent with established science rather than reaching for it.

The Gurwitsch glass/quartz discrimination is the kind of clean empirical signature that the framework's substrate-coupling claims should produce. The medium that blocks the effect (glass) blocks UV photons specifically; the medium that transmits (quartz) transmits UV. The mechanism is photon-based, not chemical or mechanical, by direct test.

The Popp DNA-as-exciplex theory provides a candidate molecular substrate for H_BP1 — DNA as the molecular carrier of coherent EM information storage and emission. This connects to H_BP12 (Coherent Body) — health/disease as substrate-coherence dynamics — by providing a candidate molecular implementation of what the framework was treating as substrate-level abstraction.

The hormesis framing is consistent with the framework's prediction of dose-dependent therapeutic-vs-harmful operating regimes. Low-dose substrate-coupling support vs high-dose catastrophic decoupling is structurally what H_BP9 (catastrophic-coupling clinical signature) and H_BP12 (substrate-coherence as health axis) jointly predict; the radiation-medicine subfield has independently developed this dose-dependent picture under its own empirical pressures.

**Where we diverge / hedges to maintain.**
- "Biophoton signaling is established science" needs careful statement. It is established within the radiation-medicine and Russian-lineage research traditions. It has not penetrated mainstream Western cell biology to the same extent; many cell biologists would not consider it foundational. The framework should be honest that biophoton signaling is empirically supported but is not yet textbook-mainstream.
- Popp's DNA-as-exciplex theory is one candidate molecular implementation; it is not the only one (mitochondrial sources, lipid peroxidation byproducts, etc. all contribute). Treating Popp's theory as load-bearing requires more evidence than this review provides.
- Per audit catch C1.4: Tong's review does not test the Coherence Principle or any of the H_BPx hypotheses. The framework recognizes biophoton signaling as the substrate H_BP1 was reaching for; this is post-hoc structural alignment, not framework-test.
- RIBE mediating mechanisms include biophoton signaling *alongside* exosomes, ROS/RNS, ATP, microRNAs, and many others. Biophoton signaling is one channel among several; framing it as the primary mediating channel overstates its established role in the bystander-effect literature.
- The mainstream cavity-QED framing is theoretical-framework-proposed, not empirically confirmed at the biological scale. Connecting Popp's framework to Huang et al. 2026's cavity-QED N-photon correlations is structurally suggestive but spans many orders of magnitude in coupling strength and energy scale.
- Hormesis remains a contested empirical claim in radiation medicine. The framework's reading of hormesis as dose-dependent substrate-coherence dynamics is one interpretation among several within an actively-debated subfield.
