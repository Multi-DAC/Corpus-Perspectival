# Hossain — New 5D Multi-Brane Proposition

## What it claims

Argues the standard ΛCDM model is "an unsustainable patchwork." Based on comprehensive review of cosmological anomalies (Hubble tension, dark energy expansion behavior, etc.), Hossain proposes:

- **5-dimensional model with expanding 4D multi-branes**
- The observed universe exists on a **single brane** generated in the aftermath of the Big Bang
- **Dark matter and dark energy serve merely as the gravitational and expanding manifestations of adjacent branes interacting across the fifth dimension**

Effectively: what we see as "DM" and "DE" are not separate sectors at all — they are the projected effects of *other branes* in the 5D bulk on our brane. The cosmological anomalies (Hubble tension, etc.) become artifacts of the brane-brane interaction structure rather than mysteries requiring new dark-sector physics.
