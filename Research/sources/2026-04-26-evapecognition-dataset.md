## What it reports

The **EVApeCognition Dataset** aggregates 262 experimental datasets from 150 publications spanning 2001-2020 from the Wolfgang Köhler Primate Research Centre, Leipzig. It includes data from ~100 institutions and >80 individual great apes (chimpanzees, bonobos, others in Hominidae). Data was standardized and subjected to internal review before publication on GitHub.

The release is **infrastructure, not a specific cognitive finding.** The aggregation enables future work on developmental questions, individual differences, and cross-species comparison that "are impossible to answer with single studies." Stated comparative goal: identify "exactly which mental skills are uniquely human" against a backbone of standardized ape data.
