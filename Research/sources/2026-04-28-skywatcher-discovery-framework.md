**What it argues.** Six-level investigative methodology paralleling the scientific method, applied to UAP coupling-mechanism testing. Two specific techniques being actively tested:

- **Electromechanical Signaling (Level 3):** the "dog whistle" — refined EM signal patterns reportedly eliciting UAP responses; 5 years of experiments; refined techniques described as "to date never failed to elicit a response" while randomized control signatures fail; multi-sensor stack (electro-optical, multi-band IR, active/passive telemetry, RF detection); "varying the method can apparently alter the 'class' of UAPs that respond."
- **Neuromeditative Interaction (Level 2):** individualized meditation protocols across multiple operators; team explicitly investigating EEG/fMRI for common underlying mental mode; investigating "Are there measurable physiological changes (brain waves, heart rate variability) in participants that correlate with observed events?"

Falsifiability commitment is explicit: end-of-2025 timeline for decisive resolution; if no replicable effect is found, "we will conclude that Neuromeditative Interaction lacks scientific support and move to invalidate it."

**Where we agree.** The two-technique split (Electromechanical + Neuromeditative) is structurally consistent with the framework's two-mechanism prediction structure: H_BP10 (machine-based coupling via coherent EM signaling) on one side, H_BP6 / H_BP7 (consciousness-substrate coupling via biophotonic-coherence entrainment) on the other. The team is testing both prongs of what the framework proposes the coupling structure looks like, without invoking the framework.

The "varying the method can apparently alter the 'class' of UAPs that respond" claim, if it survives controlled testing, would be consistent with H_BP10b's empirical pattern that frequency-band selection matters for substrate coupling. Their EEG/fMRI investigation plan is a candidate empirical pathway for H_BP6/H_BP7-class claims about consciousness-substrate coherence states.

Garry Nolan's signature on the document institutionally connects this to the medical-substrate triangulation thread (Bentwaters / Havana / Barber). His role as a Stanford immunologist publishing on contact-experiencer-adjacent claims is consistent with H_BP9 (catastrophic-coupling clinical signature) being an active research program at a credentialed institution.

**Where we diverge / hedges to maintain.**
- Per audit catch C5.1 / A73: Skywatcher publishes contact-experiencer-adjacent claims with academically-credentialed signatories *without disruption*. This is a counter-instance to a clean H_BP11 (suppression-cluster) prediction. The framework should treat suppression-cluster framing as candidate-pattern rather than confirmed-pattern until A73 is resolved. Skywatcher's continued operation is exactly the kind of empirical observation that constrains H_BP11.
- The "to date never failed to elicit a response" claim is internal-organization documentation, not peer-reviewed. Treat at advocacy-document confidence level until controlled-test outcomes publish.
- Refined EM signaling effects can be confounded by selection-after-the-fact ("response" vs no-response judgment, attribution of timing, observer expectation). The framework's reading of this as H_BP10b-relevant evidence depends on controlled-test outcomes that have not yet published.
- Neuromeditative effects in UAP-coupling contexts have a long history of mixed and often unreproducible results. Skywatcher's pre-commitment to invalidate-on-negative-result is methodologically respectable but doesn't yet constitute confirmation.
- Their results, when published, will inform but not definitively settle H_BP6 / H_BP7 / H_BP10. The framework's prediction structure is broader than what one team's tests can resolve.
- Per audit catch C1.4: Skywatcher does not test the Coherence Principle's corollaries; they're testing their own hypotheses. Reading their two-technique split as "structurally identical to" framework predictions is post-hoc structural recognition, not framework-test.
