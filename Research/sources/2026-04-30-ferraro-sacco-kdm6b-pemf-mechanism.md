# Ferraro-Sacco KDM6B PEMF Mechanism

## What it claims

Post-market surveillance data on 81 patients treated with the SynthéXer Class IIa PEMF device for severe chronic pain. Clinical results:

- **Mean NPRS (Numerical Pain Rating Scale) plummeted 8.07 ± 1.65 → 1.79 ± 1.67 post-treatment**
- 6.28-point reduction
- **Cohen's d = 3.1** (immense statistical effect size)
- **p < 0.001**
- **98% of cohort achieved ≥2-point pain reduction** (rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, discopathies)
- **Zero reported adverse effects**

## The mechanistic hypothesis

The **KDM6B-Mediated Modulation Mechanism**:

1. Low-intensity PEMF: **0.001-2 Gauss**
2. Highly specific frequency ranges: **10 Hz to 10 kHz**
3. Direct upregulation of **KDM6B (also known as JMJD3)** — a targeted histone demethylase
4. KDM6B enzymatically removes **H3K27me3** (repressive tri-methylation) marks from chromatin structure
5. Chromatin "opens" → rapid expression of **IL-4 + IL-10 anti-inflammatory cytokines**
6. Macrophages transition from pro-inflammatory M1 → restorative anti-inflammatory M2 phenotype
7. Rapid + sustained pain resolution

**The key reframing claim:** PEMF is not "energy therapy" but precise epigenetic modulator.
