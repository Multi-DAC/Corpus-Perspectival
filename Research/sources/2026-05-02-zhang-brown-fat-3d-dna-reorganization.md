## Summary (from medicalxpress + WebFetch engagement)

**Mechanism documented:** cold detection → sympathetic activation → hormonal signal → rapid 3D DNA reorganization in brown fat → metabolic gene activation.

**Key carrier:** **H2A.Z histone variant** — opens packed DNA, makes regulatory regions of key metabolic genes accessible. Distant DNA stretches form loops that enable key metabolic gene switching.

**Tissue context:** Brown adipose tissue (brown fat); mouse brown fat cells + primary human brown fat cells from donors.

**Timescale:** "Very quickly" reorganize — described as rapid changes; specific quantification (seconds/minutes/hours) not in popular-science source; full PDF would specify.

**Cell-of-origin pathway:** body detects cold → nervous system releases hormone → brown fat cells start burning fuel → activation accompanied by rapid 3D DNA reorganization → metabolic genes switch on.
