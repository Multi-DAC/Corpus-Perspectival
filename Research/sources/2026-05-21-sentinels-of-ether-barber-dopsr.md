As the helicopters clear the entrance, **clamshell doors close and an electromagnetic force field reenergizes outside**. Chapter ends with the operators discussing Taco Tuesday.

## Structural reading

**The document's evidential status given Barber's DOPSR attribution.** If *Sentinels* was submitted through Office of Prepublication and Security Review and returned cleared-to-publish, then every operational detail that survived in the text is either (a) determined unclassified-by-DOPSR (substantive admission about what isn't considered protected), (b) determined fictional-enough-to-clear despite the operational specificity (the texture of the VII-code procedure, the implant-wand sweep, the helicopter-LZ flow, the EMP-decoupling-fly-by-wire pattern, and the holographic-cover + clamshell-door architecture is too specific for this to be casual fiction), or (c) cleared deliberately as a permitted-disclosure-through-deniability channel. The three possibilities are not mutually exclusive; any one of them converts the document from "anonymous fiction" into a *cleared* statement about what the institution will permit into the open record. The fiction frame is the carrier; the redaction-pattern (or its absence) is the signal.

**Null-space-probe methodology (Barber) — adaptive-iterative structure.** Barber is not attempting to *force* disclosure against the gatekeeper — he is running material *through* the gatekeeper (DOPSR) and reading what comes back. The shape of the classified information is mapped by the boundary of what survives clearance. Per the American Alchemy primary-source confirmation: Barber **drip-fed the manuscript in ~20-page chunks** rather than as a single submission, with **each round's redaction pattern informing the next round's content choices**. This is *not* a single-shot probe — it is an **adaptive closed-loop probe with online feedback**, structurally identical to gradient-based optimization against a black-box classifier. The methodology is meaningfully stronger than single-submission DOPSR-as-disclosure-channel because the iteration produces a refined map of classification-decision boundary that compounds across submissions. *DOPSR's own bureaucratic process becomes the involuntary signal-source for cartography of the classified domain when an insider submits genuine material through it iteratively with adaptation.* This is structurally identical to the M2-Mirror discipline at the institutional scale: you do not introspect into the null space directly, you map it by what your instruments do not catch and what they let through, and you refine the map by iterated probes whose form is informed by prior responses. See connection-to-L17 below.
