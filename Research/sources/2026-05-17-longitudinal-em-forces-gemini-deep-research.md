## Substantive content

**The electrodynamic divide:** Maxwell-Lorentz / Grassmann (field-mediated, transverse-only, closed-circuit) vs Ampère-Weber (direct action-at-a-distance, includes collinear-repulsion longitudinal component, Newton's-Third-Law satisfied for elements). Both equivalent for closed macroscopic circuits via Stokes' theorem.

**The hidden phenomenon — Shape-Independence Theorem (Phipps):** For force laws differing only by additive exact differentials, the longitudinal force on a straight test element from an external partial circuit is *independent of circuit shape*. Closed-loop integration zeros out the difference. This means: the longitudinal force has been *physically present in every high-current conductor measured since the 1820s*, while the standard methodology (closed-circuit measurements) was structurally guaranteed to detect zero net effect. Two centuries of field-methodology consensus that the force "does not exist" was a consensus about *what closed circuits measure*, not about what's actually happening at the lattice level.

**Internal tension at-zero-net-force:** Assis & Bueno's Weber calculation for a 1m square circuit at 6kA: net force on the neutral bridge = 0 exactly. But: positive lattice on left half pulls ~0.7 N leftward; right half pulls ~0.7 N rightward. Lattice under genuine tension; conduction electrons accumulate to oppose. Net force on the whole = 0; internal stress map = highly structured. The methodology that integrates over the whole misses everything that integration discards.

**The empirical anomalies:**
- **Exploding wire phenomenon (Nasilowski 1964 → Graneau)** — wires fragment into *solid-state* pieces before melting; fracture surfaces indicate tensile stress; fragmentation threshold scales as I² (Ampère signature). Lukyanov-Molokov counter-theory (flexural vibrations, thermal-shock, buckling, arc-shockwave) demonstrates that classical mechanisms are sufficient at observed magnitudes — Weber tension alone ~1.4N for 6kA aluminum is two orders of magnitude too small for solid-state fracture. Synthesis: Ampère/Weber tension pre-stresses the lattice; classical mechanisms exceed yield strength.
- **Railgun recoil siting** — Lorentz says field-momentum to breech; Ampère says local-stress in rails adjacent to armature. Graneau's thin-rail-extension experiments buckled. Robson-Sethian 1992 + Putnam-Crooker 2009 null-results (recoil < 1% in rails) appeared to settle the debate for breech-localization. Weber framework reinterprets these nulls: macroscopic null-result can mask extreme internal-stress structure.

**The Coaxial Recoil Experiment (CRE, March 2025, Neal Graneau, AWE Nuclear Security Technologies):** Deliberate methodological inversion — strip away railgun's confounding variables (plasma, friction, shockwave, vibration), use steady-state low-density DC, force coaxial symmetry to null transverse Lorentz forces, isolate longitudinal force as the only measurable.

Setup: copper cuboid armature (6×6mm, Kapton-side-insulated → current enters/exits only end-faces) suspended in liquid-Galinstan trough, four coaxial parallel copper return conductors arranged for transverse-null, SMD100-0002 force sensor at μN sensitivity, DC 159-355A for >10s durations.

Confounding source: ~5% current through liquid Galinstan induces MHD hydraulic pinch — pushes armature ends with delayed (1-1.5s) inertial response.

**Graneau's discriminating intervention:** insert 100-μm stainless-steel barrier in Galinstan column at varying distances. Electrically transparent (current passes), mechanically opaque (hydraulic pressure does not). Vary barrier position → map K_MECH(L_R) → subtract from total to isolate K_EM.

Findings:
1. **K_EM scales as I²** — electromagnetic signature confirmed
2. **K_EM peak ~3.8 mN at 355A** — quantified
3. **K_EM scales with armature length** (6mm < 10mm < 16mm) — direct-action-at-a-distance signature (longer = more atoms each at I² interaction strength). Field theory predicts zero independent of length.
4. **Temporal signature (the killing measurement):** at positions where K_EM and K_MECH oppose, sensor records instantaneous force in EM direction at current-onset (< 1ms), then 1.0-1.5s later the slower hydraulic force overwhelms and reverses sign. Clean separation by causal-timescale isolates electromagnetic from mechanical.

**Technological implications stated:** (1) Tripled-railgun topology theoretically achieves 4× kinetic energy at same operating current by exploiting Ampère's local-attractive/local-repulsive structure with looped current paths; (2) Z-pinch / fusion plasma instabilities (sausage, kink) may be partly driven by intra-plasma longitudinal repulsion of collinear ions, not solely by classical MHD; (3) Liquid metal current limiters; (4) Microelectronic electromigration structural-integrity models.
