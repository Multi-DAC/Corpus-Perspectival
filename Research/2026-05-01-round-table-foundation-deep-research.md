# The Round Table Foundation (1948–1958) — Institutional Deep Research

*Andrija Puharich's parapsychology research foundation: founding context, funding network, intelligence adjacencies, closure, surviving records. The originating institutional node of the Puharich → Lab Nine → SRI Stargate → TTSA receiver-pattern research lineage.*

## Why this matters for the framework

The Round Table Foundation is **the only node in the entire 78-year Puharich → TTSA lineage where public-philanthropy and covertly-sourced military-grade instrumentation funding were institutionally entangled in a single budget.** By SRI Stargate the streams had separated (ONR/CIA/DIA on one side, philanthropic on the other). By TTSA philanthropy is publicly auditable. **Glen Cove 1948-1958 is the only entanglement point.**

This makes Round Table the **prototype institutional case for LC9** — receiver-integrity-conditions and manufactured-content as dual to manufactured-null-space. The moment in the lineage where the receiver-integrity question is most operationally contested is at the *origin* of the institutional stream the framework tracks. Everything downstream inherits from that origin.

Additionally: the Foundation's full legal name was **Round Table Foundation *of Electrobiology*** — from inception, the framing was that telepathy / clairvoyance are *physical electromagnetic signal-transmission processes amenable to instrumentation*. This aligns directly with the framework's H_BP10 (frequency-matching matters) + EM-substrate work in the Coherent Body cluster. **The Foundation's original operational framing was substrate-coherence-via-EM-coupling 75 years before the framework landed it.**

## Founding and legal structure

Established by Andrija Puharich (Henry Karl Puharich) in **1948**, initially in **Camden, Maine**, in a former WWII Navy storage barn (prior contents not publicly documented — a detail later researchers have flagged as suggestive of pre-existing Navy interest in the property). When the barn became unavailable in 1949, Puharich's patron group purchased the **"Warrenton" estate in Glen Cove (Rockport), Maine** — a 65-acre property anchored by a 45-room house — for **$35,000**, and the Foundation operated from that address until its 1958 dissolution.

Puharich's trajectory from Northwestern Medical School (MD 1947) to founding a parapsychology laboratory eighteen months later is unusually compressed. The proximate cause was his December 1947 visit to Maine to see violinist Zlatko Baloković and his wife Joyce Borden Baloković — a dairy heiress and sister of writer Mary Borden, with strong interests in mysticism. Joyce became his first patron and the social bridge to the eastern-establishment funding network. By June 1949, just over a year after founding, Puharich had received approximately **$13,000 in grants** and had three laboratory assistants on payroll.

The Foundation's stated purpose was the study of **the physico-chemical (specifically electromagnetic) basis of paranormal phenomena**. From inception, the program's framing was that telepathy, clairvoyance, and related phenomena were *physical* signal-transmission processes amenable to instrumentation — Faraday cages, pulse generators, drug interventions — rather than purely psychological or spiritual.

## Government and intelligence adjacencies

**Firm / FOIA-documented:**

1. **CIA Technical Services Staff (TSS) interest from February 1952** — predates Pentagon briefing by 9 months. **Robert Lashbrook's memorandum to John Mulholland** explicitly evaluating Faraday-cage potential for psychic-warfare research is part of MK-ULTRA / TSS document corpus released through the 1977 Senate hearings and subsequent FOIA tranches.
2. **Army active-duty conscription as direct consequence of Pentagon briefing** (Nov 24, 1952 briefing → Nov 25 active-duty request → Feb 26, 1953 induction)
3. **Edgewood Arsenal Chemical Biological Center service, 1953–1955**, including project officially described in declassified personnel records as **"to locate a drug that might enhance ESP"**
4. **John Mulholland's November 11, 1953 critical report** "A New Type of Experiment in Parapsychology" assessing Round Table Foundation work — CIA stage-magician evaluation. Document exists; full text remains partially redacted.
5. **The CIA's Christopher "Kit" Green**, much later (1974–1975, OSI), formally evaluated parapsychology research including Puharich's Geller protocols at SRI — the bureaucratic-continuity link between Glen Cove–era CIA interest and the Stargate program proper.

**Claimed, plausible, but not document-anchored:**

6. **"CIA Office of Security interest dating to 1952"** — Picknett & Prince + VISUP claim. OS connection runs through ARTICHOKE (which OS administered briefly late-1951 to September 1952 before TSS took it over). **No FOIA document directly establishes OS sponsorship of the Foundation;** inference rests on (a) the unaccounted $100,000 cage funding, (b) ARTICHOKE timing overlap, (c) Morse Allen's June 24, 1953 mushroom-cultivation meeting in Toughkenamon, PA running concurrent with the Glen Cove "Nine" séance (June 27, 1953).
7. **Dr. Stephen Aldrich link** — graduated Northwestern Medical School same year as Puharich (1947), later headed CIA's Office of Research and Development, funded SRI remote-viewing research. Career-trajectory parallel documented; institutional collaboration inferred.
8. **Frank Olson connection** — Olson and Puharich had documented mid-1952 confrontation at Chemical Corps Biological Division briefing. Olson called Puharich's work "frightened schoolboy." Olson died under contested circumstances November 1953. Ira Einhorn later made unverifiable claim that Puharich's drug research was implicated. **Speculative.**

**Bottom line:** the *interest* of US intelligence in Puharich's program is fully documented from February 1952 forward. The *funding* relationship is inferred but not proven. The Round Table was almost certainly *informed* by intelligence interest; whether it was institutionally *captured* by it is genuinely open.

## Closure and successor institutions

- **1958:** Round Table Foundation dissolved. "Lost much of its funding." Patron generation aged out; principal donors died or shifted philanthropy; *Sacred Mushroom* publication marked public-facing closure of original research arc.
- **1958 – April 1971: 13-year "wilderness" period.** No single institutional successor. Brazilian fieldwork with Arigó (psychic surgery); Mexico-based mushroom and shamanic studies; medical-device patent work; loose Belk Psychic Research Foundation affiliation; Nine communications continued intermittently.
- **April 1971:** Puharich pivots full-time back to parapsychology. Within months Uri Geller phase begins; SRI Puthoff/Targ work begins under separate auspices in 1972.
- **Lab Nine (early 1970s – 1978)** at 87 Hawkes Avenue, Ossining, NY. Founded jointly by Puharich, **Sir John Whitmore** (British aristocrat / former racing driver), and **Phyllis Schlemmer** (Florida-based psychic). Nine channelings continued — first via "Bobby Horne" (Daytona cook pseudonym), then with Schlemmer as authorized channel for "Tom." Funders included **Bronfman family** members and **Baron DiPauli**; **Gene Roddenberry** orbited the group and incorporated material into Star Trek.
- **1978:** Lab Nine ends. Arson attack on Ossining estate; Puharich flees to Mexico claiming CIA persecution. Schlemmer + Whitmore continue independently — eventually publishing *The Only Planet of Choice* (1992).
- **January 3, 1995:** Puharich died in Dobson, NC.

**Continuity:** Glen Cove (1948–1958) → ~13-year diffuse period → Lab Nine Ossining (~1971–1978). One continuous research stream institutionally with one substantial gap.

## Critical analysis (Picknett & Prince, *The Stargate Conspiracy*, 1999)

The most rigorous *institutional* analysis of the Round Table — itself not a conventional skeptical text. Picknett & Prince take the phenomena seriously enough to argue that the **Nine material was *constructed* by intelligence-community psychological-warfare operations** rather than dismissing it as fantasy. Their critique structure is *exactly* what LC9 names:

- Puharich's electronic experiments seem instrumentally aimed at *producing* hearing-voices states rather than testing pre-existing telepathic capacity
- Session transcripts show **frequent leading questions** to hypnotized subjects, methodologically fatal for any claim of independent communication
- The Nine's content correlates suspiciously well with what a 1950s psychological-warfare operation focused on producing controllable mediumistic claims would generate
- Funding-network secrecy plus timing of military induction points to sponsorship relationship rather than independent civilian research

**Whether they're right is a separate question from whether the hypothesis structure matters.** The hypothesis structure IS LC9 — manufactured-content as dual to manufactured-null-space, with the Round Table as the prototype institutional case where natural-receiver-pattern-research and state-actor-information-injection are most plausibly entangled in the same institutional budget.

**Two findings that complicate the "manufactured content" reading:**

- **D.G. Vinod was a Hindu sage / Sanskrit scholar** Puharich met **by chance in NYC December 1951** before either had any documented intelligence connection — Vinod independently entered trance and channeled "the Nine" December 31, 1952. He returned to India after months of sessions. The Vinod-as-state-asset reading requires explaining how Vinod was either pre-positioned or post-recruited; neither is documented.
- **The Garrett Faraday-cage results (89/91)** were obtained at Hammond's Castle in Gloucester, MA in March 1951 — *before* the documented CIA interest emerges. The originating empirical signal is not obviously contaminated by intelligence apparatus; it's what attracted the intelligence interest.

**So the Picknett-Prince hypothesis fits the *Council of Nine session content* better than it fits the *originating Faraday-cage empirical work*.** The receiver-pattern phenomenon being studied may have been real; the *Council material specifically* is where state-actor influence is most plausible. **This is exactly the LC9 distinction:** receiver-pattern is real AND can be selectively compromised; manufactured-content can occupy a real receiver-pattern channel.
