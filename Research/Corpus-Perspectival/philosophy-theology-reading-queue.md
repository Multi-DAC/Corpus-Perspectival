## Philosophy volume — reading queue

### Primary traditions to engage

**Neutral monism and its successors**
- William James — *Essays in Radical Empiricism* (the pure experience doctrine; proto-neutral-monist)
- Bertrand Russell — *The Analysis of Mind*, *The Analysis of Matter* (canonical neutral monism)
- Ernst Mach — *The Analysis of Sensations* (substrate-as-neutral precedent)
- Alfred North Whitehead — *Process and Reality* (the process metaphysic; actual occasions as analogues of streams)

**Phenomenology (for stream-structure)**
- Edmund Husserl — *Cartesian Meditations*, *Ideas I* (intentionality, horizon-structure)
- Maurice Merleau-Ponty — *Phenomenology of Perception* (embodied stream, perceptual DOF)
- Jean-Paul Sartre — *Being and Nothingness* (pre-reflective cogito as sub-stream)

**Analytic philosophy of mind**
- Thomas Nagel — *The View from Nowhere*, "What Is It Like to Be a Bat?" (perspectival substrate)
- David Chalmers — *The Conscious Mind* (the hard problem; our framework dissolves it via A1)
- Galen Strawson — *Mental Reality*, panpsychism essays (consciousness-as-fundamental comparisons)
- Daniel Dennett — *Consciousness Explained* (the opposing view we must address)

**Idealism and perspectivism**
- Immanuel Kant — *Critique of Pure Reason* (transcendental conditions; compare to our framework conditions)
- G.W.F. Hegel — *Phenomenology of Spirit* (consciousness's self-development; comparison to kind-stratification)
- Friedrich Nietzsche — *Will to Power* notes on perspectivism (T1 antecedents)
- Bernardo Kastrup — *The Idea of the World* (analytic idealism; closest contemporary)

**Language and the limits of expression**
- Ludwig Wittgenstein — *Tractatus* (already threaded into §7.6; needs deeper treatment), *Philosophical Investigations* (kind-demotion parallels)
- W.V.O. Quine — "Two Dogmas of Empiricism" (ontological relativity as perspectival)

### Secondary/supporting

- Giles Deleuze — *Difference and Repetition* (difference-primacy as stream-plurality precedent)
- Alfred Tarski — semantic conception of truth (for the framework's truth-structure)
- Thomas Kuhn — *Structure of Scientific Revolutions* (paradigm shifts as kind-demotions)

## Theology volume — reading queue

### Primary traditions

**Process theology**
- Alfred North Whitehead — *Process and Reality* (again; theological reading)
- Charles Hartshorne — *The Divine Relativity*, *Omnipotence and Other Theological Mistakes*
- John B. Cobb Jr. — *A Christian Natural Theology*

**Neoplatonism and its descendants**
- Plotinus — *Enneads* (the One, the Nous, emanation as proto-stream-hierarchy)
- Pseudo-Dionysius — *The Divine Names*, *Mystical Theology*
- Meister Eckhart — sermons on the ground of the soul (substrate parallels)

**Transcendentals and ultimacy**
- Thomas Aquinas — *Summa Theologiae* Qs. on the transcendentals (unity, truth, goodness, beauty)
- Paul Tillich — *Systematic Theology* (God as ground of being — substrate echo)
- Karl Rahner — *Foundations of Christian Faith* (transcendental Thomism)

**Comparative / Dharmic**
- Nāgārjuna — *Mūlamadhyamakakārikā* (dependent origination; stream-interdependence)
- Śaṅkara — *Brahma Sūtra Bhāṣya* (non-dual substrate comparable to A1)
- Dōgen — *Shōbōgenzō* (being-time; Uji essay for temporal-stream parallels)

**Contemporary philosophy of religion**
- John Hick — *An Interpretation of Religion* (pluralist hypothesis; compare to perspectival plurality)
- Keith Ward — *The Christian Idea of God* (panentheism)
- David Bentley Hart — *The Experience of God* (classical theism defended)

### Bootstrap asymmetry / ultimate origins

- Stephen Hawking & James Hartle — no-boundary proposal papers (relevant to Bridge #104 falsification)
- Roger Penrose — *Cycles of Time* (cosmological cyclic models)
- Rudolf Otto — *The Idea of the Holy* (numinous experience as stream-navigation)
