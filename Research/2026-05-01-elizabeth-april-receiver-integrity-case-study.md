# Elizabeth April — *Asking for Squares* E91 — Receiver-Integrity Case Study

*The first operational LC9 diagnostic application: receiver-integrity-conditions discriminating a contemporary contactee/channeler case in real-time. Source: 7476-line podcast transcript (~18K spoken words; Cody Blue + Tanya D, hosts; episode 91 of *Asking for Squares*).*

## Why this matters for the framework

Elizabeth April is **contemporary, active, and operating in the same general territory** as the historical receiver-pattern figures the framework engages (Cayce, Puharich, Council of Nine, Ra, Bashar/Anka, Bledsoe). She self-describes as a "certified double blind tested remote viewer" with abduction experiences and Galactic Federation contact. She gives specific predictions for 2026 with date-anchoring.

LC9's diagnostic value depends on its ability to discriminate cases. EA is a **clean test case** because she sits *middle-of-spectrum* — neither obviously rigorous (Ra protocols, Mishlove evidential standards) nor obviously compromised (Manson, Burisch). LC9's integrity-conditions discriminate her position cleanly without categorical judgment. **This case demonstrates the diagnostic working as designed.**

## Most structurally significant finding

**Cosmology architecture matches Ra/Bashar/Cannon almost exactly without invoking them by name.** Twelve densities, oscillation between unity and separation, polarity choice (her higher-vs-lower-vibrational-DNA maps onto service-to-self vs service-to-others), star-seed volunteers, harvest/timeline-split. **Dolores Cannon is the only explicit lineage acknowledgment.** No Ra, no Bashar, no Cayce, no Council of Nine, no Puharich.

Two competing readings, empirically discriminable:

- **Reading A:** She's tapping the same archetypal substrate other receivers report → genuine convergence is signal of cross-receiver coherence (Network B's vocabulary-consistency claim being load-bearing)
- **Reading B:** She's absorbed the broader New Age channeler corpus and reflects it back → cultural-substrate confabulation operating in receiver-mode

**The "Dimensions are densities" collapse is diagnostic for B over A.** Ra sharply distinguishes density (consciousness-development octave) from dimension (geometric/spatial). Bashar likewise. EA collapses them, indicating she's working from *general cultural absorption* of the densities-language rather than from inside Ra's specific framework. A receiver tapping the same substrate as Ra would more likely preserve the distinction; a receiver reflecting the corpus would compress it.

**Not dispositive** — could go either way — but the diagnostic *exists* and lands in a particular direction.
