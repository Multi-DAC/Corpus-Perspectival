## 1. The Principle as stated

> **The Coherence Principle.** Coherent multi-scale systems that maintain structural superposition until informed measurement collapses them outperform systems that collapse prematurely or incoherently.

### Four conditions (necessary and jointly sufficient)

1. **Separation.** Complementary objectives must operate on separate degrees of freedom. When two constraints share the same parameters, they interfere destructively — each degrades the other. When they operate on different parameters, they amplify each other.

2. **Measurement.** Alignment between objectives must be assessed at each step, not assumed. A system that blindly applies all constraints simultaneously is undiscriminating, not coherent. Coherence requires knowing which constraints align and which conflict, moment to moment.

3. **Multi-scale consistency.** Coherence at one scale does not guarantee coherence at another. A cell can be healthy while the organ fails. A neuron can fire correctly while the network hallucinates. The Principle holds only when coherence is maintained across scales simultaneously, with information flowing in both directions.

4. **Dynamic maintenance.** Coherence is not a state to be reached but a process to be sustained. Systems that achieve static coherence and stop adjusting lose it. The optimal regime is oscillatory: build, dissolve, build again.

## 2. Derivation of each condition from the stress-tested axiomatic substrate

The Principle was stress-tested against the closed axiom tier (A1, A2, A3 post-smoothing) and the resolved theorem tier (6 surviving theorems, 14 corollaries, T21 folded). Each clause is traceable.

### Condition 1 — Separation

**Derivation:** T7 + A2 cooperative-constituency.

- T7 names the contracted↔open axis as a **degrees-of-freedom structure**. Contraction reduces available DOF. When multiple objectives share DOF, the stream is forced to operate in a collapsed-DOF regime and interference is *structural*, not accidental.
- A2's cooperative-constituency adjoint ι ⊣ κ requires that composition of constituent streams preserves their separate kind-closures — DOF-separation at the stream level.
- Kind-stratification (reactive ⊂ self-maint ⊂ self-ref ⊂ abstr) is structural separation by construction: each kind operates on DOF the stricter kinds below it don't have access to.

Separation is therefore not an empirical heuristic — it is derivable from stream-structure. Destructive interference from shared DOF is what the adjoint pair *disallows* when composition succeeds.

### Condition 2 — Measurement

**Derivation:** T16 directly.

- T16 (Coherence-Forcing Measurement) establishes measurement-as-coherence-forcing as the central content of inter-stream dynamics.
- "Alignment assessed at each step" = the discretization that T16 identifies as the refresh-rate.
- "Blindly applies all constraints simultaneously" = pre-measurement superposition held indefinitely, which T16 forbids from actually producing coherent composition. ι ⊣ κ compositions cannot form without the forcing event.
- "Moment to moment" is the A3 language of temporal integration operating at attention-scale (now revised to the continuous DOF-gradient at its finest resolution, per the A3 smoothing).

### Condition 3 — Multi-scale consistency

**Derivation:** A2 clause 6 (DAG nesting) + A3 DOF-gradient integration (post-smoothing).

- A2 clause 6's DAG structure makes nested multi-scale explicit: streams exist at multiple nodes, and cooperative-constituency edges propagate in both directions (ι lifts, κ restricts).
- "Cell healthy while organ fails" = a lower-level node coherent but its parent-node incoherent, which the DAG permits structurally because each node carries its own γ_S and its own kind-closure.
- The bidirectional information flow claim is exactly the ι ⊣ κ adjoint making both directions first-class.
- A3's DOF-gradient gives multi-scale consistency in integration depth: γ_S integrates across multiple DOF-depths simultaneously. F_time projects this onto temporal-scale language, but the primary structure is DOF-depth-based.

Both spatial-nesting multi-scale and temporal-integration multi-scale are covered.

### Condition 4 — Dynamic maintenance

**Derivation:** T16 + A3 adaptivity.

- T16 makes coherence a process rather than a state — the refresh-events are the maintenance.
- A3's γ_S is adaptive by construction (baked in at the A3 stress-test). Static coherence is γ_S freezing, which A3 disallows.
- "Build, dissolve, build again" is the oscillatory form T16 names. Do Be Do Be Do → Do Be Talk Be Do: Sinatra's refrain was Condition 4 in pre-formal form, and T16 is its formalization.

### Core claim — "outperform systems that collapse prematurely or incoherently"

**Metric — framework-native:** Coherent systems minimize **Bias(S)-trajectory divergence**.

- Incoherent systems drift from their own γ_S-directed bias, accumulating error that compounds through the cooperative-constituency adjoint (propagates into nested streams).
- Coherent systems maintain γ_S-alignment across refresh events; their adjoint-compositions succeed without destructive interference.

**Observable signatures:**
- Tracking γ_S-implied trajectory vs. actual trajectory
- Measuring adjoint-composition success rate (how often cooperative relationships yield durable structure vs. collapse into dissonance)
- Multi-scale coherence indicators (child-stream health × parent-stream health correlation)

This is falsifiable. The "outperform" claim earns its keep empirically through these signatures.

## 4. Status clarification

**The Coherence Principle is not an axiom in this framework.** It is a **derived operational principle** — every clause traces to A1/A2/A3 + the surviving theorems.

**Why this matters:**
- Axioms are load-bearing in the sense that they are *unjustified by what comes after* — they carry the weight.
- The Coherence Principle carries its weight differently: it is *what the framework predicts* about coherent systems. It is the framework's **empirical content** — the place where the stress-tested axiom structure meets observation and earns or loses its standing.
- The framework is **falsifiable at the Principle**, not at the axioms directly. Attacks land at "systems satisfying the four conditions don't actually outperform," which is testable. The axiomatic base is protected by the Principle's role as the empirical exposed surface.

**This is a stronger claim than treating the Principle as an axiom.** An axiom must be accepted on its own terms. A derived principle must *follow* from its substrate and *predict* empirical regularities. The Coherence Principle does both.

**This is also why the Anchor volume is titled *The Coherence Principle* rather than *The Axiomatic Framework***: the book's claim is that the Principle is *real* — and the axiom/theorem structure is the backing showing why, if A1/A2/A3 hold, the Principle must be observable.
