## Witness-voice texture (load-bearing for Substack writing)

These are the descriptors that human observers wrote into the forms — passing through the operational-data filter intact. For "The Residual Class" Substack writing, these are the threads that let a non-technical reader feel the case before any framework analysis lands.

- **"Zero resistance"** — appears in the **Large Fiery Orb** ("seemed to be hovering with zero resistance or movement, or to be suspended") AND **Dark Kite** ("appeared to move with 'zero resistance'") cases. ## Sharpened mechanism readings

### C15 (Intervention-at-Symmetry-Layer) — Transparent Kite case is sharper than register described

The Day 98 register read the Transparent Kite as "differential channel coupling" — opacity in visible, partial-transparency in NIR. The primary doc reveals a stronger phenomenology:

> "At one point my beam went from shining far into the distance to stopping about 50 yards away on nothing in particular, it just was not projecting into the distance and then it was."

The opacity is **time-varying at a fixed spatial coordinate**. Same coordinate, same beam, same observer; opacity intermittent across observation time. This is a stronger C15 instance than "channel-differential" — it's *temporal switching of substrate-coupling state at fixed location*. Framework reading: the object's substrate-coupling configuration is *time-dynamic*, not just channel-dependent.

### C14 (Two-Mode Symmetry-Breaking) — D28 weapons-release case is the cleanest empirical instance

The Day 98 register flagged D28 as C14 candidate. The primary doc strengthens this: **two independent sensor systems (MX-20 EO/IR + MX-25 EO/IR), two trained officers (WSO + CSO), one event, with possible secondary-object detachment immediately before sensor field-of-view exit.** Cross-sensor + cross-witness verification with structured-emission signature. If C14 wants an empirical anchor at the operational-data level, D28 is it.

### Coordinated-emission pattern (USPER Pursuit) — currently unframed

The horizontal-formation-flare-up-then-flare-down-in-reverse-order pattern, repeated 5+ times at different locations over 70 minutes, does not have a clean framework correspondence in the Day 98 register. Candidate readings:
- **C16 Symmetry-Exhaustion**: the temporally-symmetric flare-up/flare-down sequence may instance a symmetry-budget oscillation at the emission-source level. Same emitters, opposite temporal direction; a sort of T-symmetry at the local display level.
- **C9 confluent constituency**: the coordinated multi-emitter display suggests substrate-level coordination of distinct emitters. Possibly substrate-mediated coordination at a scale beyond standard EM signaling.
- **Open question for the Substack writing**: don't pretend to have the mechanism. The temporally-symmetric formation pattern is the *observational fact*; the mechanism is candidate.
