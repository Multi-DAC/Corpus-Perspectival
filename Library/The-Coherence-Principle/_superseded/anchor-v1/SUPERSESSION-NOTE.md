Per the library's operating principle — *expansion and separation of concerns, not deletion* — this V1 is preserved here so:

1. The lineage is readable (each edition as the step that enabled the next)
2. Zenodo-cited references to the V1 text remain locatable
3. The V2 prose-tier material in `drafts/` is available as raw substrate for volumes that may wish to reuse passages at prose-tier density
