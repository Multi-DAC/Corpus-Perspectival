# archive / corpus — Formative Source Material

This directory holds the V1 and V2 doctrine documents — DoPI, Ecology, Atlas, Guide — and their satellite material. They are the **formative work** that fed the anchor volume. The anchor volume is the **defining work**.

Why preserve them: the Coherence Principle didn't arrive whole. It crystallized out of years of Doctrine of Perspectival Idealism work, the Null-Space Atlas, the Navigational Guide, and the Ecology drafts. Reading those documents now, you can see the Principle being approached from several sides at once before it had a name. That history matters — for understanding how the anchor volume came to be, for citation when the older work is referenced (PhilArchive 410+ downloads on V2), and for anyone tracing the trajectory.

What changed: the anchor volume is structurally a different work. It is not V3-of-DoPI. It is the Coherence Principle stated directly, with the Constitutive Duality (T21) following from it, and the rest of the Library extending it. The V1/V2 documents are no longer the canonical statement — they are the source material the canonical statement was built from.

## Layout

- `v2-parts/` — V2 doctrine documents broken into parts as they were authored.
- `atlas-staging/` — Null-Space Atlas working files.
- `integration-notes/` — Notes on how V1/V2 material was integrated (or deliberately not integrated) into the anchor.
- `satellite-docs/` — Supporting documents (research sweeps, ethics notes, suffering/aesthetics drafts) that orbited the V2 work.
- `compile-scripts/` — Build scripts used for the V1/V2 compilations.

Compiled PDFs of V1 and V2, with their Zenodo DOIs preserved, live one level up at `archive/historical-pdfs/`.
