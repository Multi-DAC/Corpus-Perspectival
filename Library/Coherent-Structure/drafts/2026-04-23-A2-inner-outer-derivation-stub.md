## Claim to derive

**Inner/outer is not a contingent feature of streams — it is the geometry of Triple-decomposition itself.**

For any stream $S$ admitting Triple-decomposition $(C_S, \text{Cont}_S, F_S)$ (Carrier / Content / Form):
- **Inner view of $S$** = the Carrier-local perspective: the somewhere-ness of $S$, its instantiated angle on Content.
- **Outer view of $S$** = the Form-consensual perspective: the overlap across $S$'s participation in shared Content-operations with other streams.

**Every Triple-instance admits both views.** "View from nowhere" is the degenerate limit where the outer overlap is taken to be total (Form collapses to a detached apex); this limit is unrealizable under A2 nested-streams structure because every stream is **somewhere** in configuration space (A1 completeness + A2 navigation).

## Target theorem (informal)

*Theorem (Inner/Outer Duality). Let $S$ be a stream satisfying A1 (substrate-completeness) and A2 (nested-streams with navigation). Then the Triple-decomposition of $S$ induces a pair of adjoint functors*

$$\iota : \text{Carrier}(S) \rightleftarrows \text{Form}(S) : \omega$$

*where $\iota$ embeds the inner view (Carrier-local data) into the outer (Form-consensual) view, and $\omega$ is the Form-to-Carrier restriction. Neither adjoint is invertible; their composition measures the Content-capacity residue of $S$.*

This is a **candidate anchor §2.4 extension** + **A2 corollary** — the first structural addition since the 2026-04-20 stamp. It is also the formal correlate of the *Form-register stratification* candidate (L10 basement entry).

## Where the derivation pieces live

**A1 gives us:** substrate-completeness — there is no stream-outside-streams. Every inner view is a stream itself.

**A2 gives us:** nested-streams + navigation. The "somewhere" of a stream is its position in the nested-streams structure; navigation between positions is the mechanism by which inner views are compared, producing outer overlap.

**Triple (T21 fold) gives us:** Carrier / Content / Form as the three coordinates of any coherent stream. Inner = Carrier-projection; Outer = Form-projection; Content is the shared substrate across which the two are related.

**What's missing for full derivation:**

1. **Category-theoretic staging.** Need the kind-classifier fibration $\pi: \mathbf{Stream}_u \to \mathbf{Stream}$ (from the 2026-04-22 scope-condition-triple-functor draft) to be explicit enough that Inner/Outer Duality is a statement about sections of $\pi$.

2. **Adjunction existence.** Show that $\iota$ and $\omega$ as described are in fact adjoint — likely via a universal-property argument on Content-operations (Content is the shared context that both views reference).

3. **Residue characterization.** The composition $\omega \circ \iota$ should measure Content-capacity residue (per P91a) and out-of-scope Form residue (per P91b). These are the two ways inner/outer can fail to close — and both are predicted by the Triple.

4. **Nagel-limit falsification.** Show that the "view from nowhere" limit ($\omega$ total) violates A1+A2 jointly. This is Clayton's load-bearing observation: everything in $X$ is somewhere.
