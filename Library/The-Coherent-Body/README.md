## What This Book Will Be

The Coherence Principle predicts that any multi-scale system under complementary constraints exhibits the same dynamics: separation of concerns, informed measurement, dynamic maintenance, multi-scale consistency. **The Coherent Body** focuses specifically on the body as a coherent dynamic system — the lived, carbon-based instance — distinct from **The Living Architecture** (which spans the broader biological hierarchy from molecules to ecosystems).

The boundary between these two volumes is not yet final and may collapse into one. The two wings exist in parallel until the right partition between them becomes obvious.
