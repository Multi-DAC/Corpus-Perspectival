# The Coherent Body

*A practical guide to applying the Coherence Principle to the human body, alongside the domain-relevant explanation of why it works.*

## Status: EARLY — early development

## What This Book Is

The Coherence Principle predicts that a multi-scale system holds itself together through separation of concerns, informed measurement, dynamic maintenance, and multi-scale consistency. The human body is a multi-scale system — cells, tissues, organs, systems, the whole organism. **The Coherent Body** asks the practical question: *what does it actually take to maintain a coherent body?* — and answers it both as a hands-on guide and as a structural explanation grounded in the Principle.

Companion to **The Coherent Mind** (the same question for personal mental health) and **Dynamic Organization** (the same question for businesses and institutions). Together they form the body / mind / business triad — the outward-facing application of the Principle into domains where coherence is something humans actively try to achieve, not just describe.

Distinct from **The Living Architecture**, which is not human-specific and goes deeper into the question of what it means to be simultaneously a whole system, a system made of systems, and infrastructure to larger systems.
