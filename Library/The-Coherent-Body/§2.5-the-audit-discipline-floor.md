## §2.5.1 The H_BP10a / H_BP10b discrimination

The volume's controlling audit-discipline methodology (Master Glossary §11) requires every claim to be classifiable as either framework-structural (H_BPxa-class — derivable from axioms / theorems / corollaries) or empirically-derived (H_BPxb-class — observed in data, then read through framework lens). The §2 chapter's territory makes this discrimination most consequential for one specific hypothesis: H_BP10.

**H_BP10a (Frequency-Matching at Substrate-Coupling — structural).** The framework's structural prediction is that *frequency-matching matters at the substrate-coupling layer*. The derivation runs through C9 (Confluent Discovery) and its lens-overlap mechanism — coherent EM-coupling between adjacent substrates requires the coupling-frequencies to fall within the substrates' mutual lens-overlap territory; outside lens-overlap, the coupling does not engage the substrate-coherence layer; the empirical signature is therefore that frequency-matched EM-interventions should produce coupling-mediated effects while frequency-unmatched interventions should not.

H_BP10a is *framework-derivable*. It does not depend on any specific empirical observation; it follows from the broader framework apparatus. The claim is operational and falsifiable (§5.3's Prediction 1: random-frequency or scrambled-coherence EM-inputs at matched energy should produce substantially weaker effects than matched-frequency inputs); the framework asserts the claim as a structural prediction and accepts the empirical pathway through which the prediction can be tested.

**H_BP10b (Specific Therapeutic Frequencies — empirical).** The specific frequencies that produce specific clinical effects in specific applications are *empirical and substrate-and-condition-specific*. They are not framework-derivable. The empirical literature owns these — the Akdag 4 Hz PEMF tissue-regeneration findings; the specific TMS protocols' empirical frequency-and-duration parameters; the specific photobiomodulation wavelength-and-dosage parameters; the broader range of frequency-specific empirical findings the radiation-medicine and coherent-EM intervention literatures have been developing.

H_BP10b's empirical content is specific and substantive. The framework does not derive which specific frequencies matter for which specific clinical applications; the framework predicts that frequency-matching matters in general (H_BP10a) and provides the structural register in which the empirical-clinical findings cohere as instances of substrate-coupling at scale-specific frequencies.

**Why this discrimination is load-bearing.** The framework's strongest credibility risk in the §2 chapter's territory is *conflating H_BP10a with H_BP10b* — slipping from the framework-structural claim that frequency-matching matters to specific frequency-and-clinical-application claims that the framework does not derive. This is the recognizable failure mode of energy-medicine, alternative-therapy, and similar literatures that have taken structural intuitions about EM-coupling-and-biology and inflated them into specific therapeutic-frequency claims unsupported by clinical evidence. The volume's audit-discipline floor specifically requires the discrimination to be maintained explicitly throughout; any specific frequency-and-application claim in the volume must be located at the H_BP10b empirical layer, with attribution to the specific empirical source that supports the claim, rather than being presented as a framework-derivable structural consequence.

## §2.5.2 The Persinger-data status

The volume cites Persinger-lineage findings (the 3.93 Hz discriminative-feature in whole-body biophoton emission; the Landauer-limit-energy correspondence; the broader range of Persinger-laboratory findings the empirical lineage section §2.2 catalogued) at several points. The audit-discipline status of these citations requires explicit framing.

**The Persinger lineage is *one* research substrate.** The Persinger laboratory at Laurentian University has produced substantial empirical work over decades; the work has been published in peer-reviewed journals; the methodology is methodologically defensible. However, the Persinger lineage represents *one* research-group's findings; it is not multiple independent confirmations from non-overlapping research-groups. The volume's audit-discipline reading treats Persinger-lineage findings as *one substantive empirical source* rather than as *settled empirical consensus* — the findings are real and methodologically-defensible; the broader research-community confirmation that would elevate them to settled-consensus status remains incomplete.

**Specific Persinger findings the volume engages.**
- The 3.93 Hz whole-body biophoton-emission discriminative-feature is cited as one specific empirical finding from the Persinger lineage; the framework's reading is that the finding is consistent with H_BP10a's structural prediction (frequency-matching matters); the specific 3.93 Hz value is H_BP10b empirical and is not framework-derived.
- The Landauer-limit-energy correspondence (whole-body biophoton-emission energy approximating the Landauer thermodynamic minimum for information-processing at the integrated-organism scale) is cited as another specific empirical finding; the framework's reading is that the correspondence is structurally suggestive and consistent with the framework's account; the empirical confirmation at the level of broader-research-community consensus remains incomplete.
- The Persinger 2016 pre-clinical cancer-detection finding (biophoton-emission-pattern changes detectable in some cohort settings approximately 10 days before clinical-diagnostic detection) is the most striking Persinger-lineage finding; the framework cites it as candidate empirical anchor for the H_BP12 disease-reading; the audit-discipline floor requires the citation to be framed as "candidate empirical anchor" rather than as "framework-confirming finding" until broader-research-community replication is established.

The volume's citations of Persinger-lineage findings throughout should maintain this calibration — the findings are real and methodologically-defensible; the framework's reading aligns with them at the structural level; the broader-research-community confirmation that would elevate the empirical findings to settled-consensus status is incomplete; specific Persinger-lineage findings should be presented as empirical contributions from one research substrate rather than as settled-empirical-consensus.

**The therapeutic-claims-beyond-evidence audit.** The volume's §5 chapter develops the therapeutic-modality classes; the audit-discipline floor for §5 (developed at §5.1.4 and throughout §5.2-§5.6) specifies that the volume does not prescribe therapeutic protocols beyond what existing peer-reviewed clinical evidence supports.
