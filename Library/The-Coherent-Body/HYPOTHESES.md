## H_BP1 — Cellular Signaling Layer

*Source: T4 (Coherence-Forcing Measurement) + C9 (Confluent-Constituency).*

**Statement.** Biophotons function as the physical implementation of inter-stream measurement-event closure between biological cells. Cell-to-cell signaling has an EM-coherence layer beyond the chemical signaling layer.

**Empirical status.** Independently established within the radiation-medicine and Russian-lineage research traditions. Tong 2024 review documents the 100-year empirical lineage from Gurwitsch's 1920s mitogenetic radiation through Popp's DNA-as-exciplex theory through modern bystander-effect mediation (RIBE). Glass-blocks-quartz-transmits discrimination locks the mechanism to UV photons specifically.

**Hedges.** "Biophoton signaling is established science" must be qualified — established within radiation-medicine; not penetrated into mainstream Western cell biology to the same extent. Biophotons are one channel among several in RIBE alongside exosomes, ROS/RNS, microRNAs, and others.

**Tractable test path.** Reanalysis of existing biophoton-laboratory data (Popp tradition + Persinger lineage + Soochow + INFN Frascati) at the single-cell-signaling scale.

## H_BP2 — Two-Mode Emission Signature

*Source: C14 (Two-Mode Symmetry-Breaking).*

**Statement.** Biophoton emission patterns should show two distinct dynamic modes (resolution mode = selection among formed states; generation mode = actualization of novel content from substrate symmetries) detectable by coherence-pattern analysis.

**Empirical status.** Untested. No published study has been designed around this specific contrast.

**Hedges.** Distinguishing two modes by emission pattern requires careful experimental design; ambient noise and metabolic variation may mask the contrast.

**Tractable test path.** In vitro coherence measurement under controlled "selection-among-alternatives" vs "creation-of-novel-state" cellular tasks.

## H_BP3 — Oscillation Requirement

*Source: C16 (Symmetry-Exhaustion / Oscillation Necessity).*

**Statement.** Biophotonic activity is oscillatory with R-mechanism cycles. Sustained high-coherence periods are followed by re-symmetrization periods. Sleep is the macro R-operator for neural biophoton coherence (recasting Tononi-Cirelli SHY in framework terms). Burnout is R-failure.

**Empirical status.** Indirect support: cell-cycle phase coupling documented in Persinger lineage; Kobayashi 1999 hippocampal-theta coupling; Dotta 2011 treatment-effect timing. Direct longitudinal sleep-wake biophoton measurement remains a future target.

**Hedges.** Recasting of Tononi-Cirelli SHY in framework terms is interpretive; the mainstream Synaptic Homeostasis Hypothesis explains the same data within standard neural-network theory.

**Tractable test path.** Longitudinal biophoton measurement across sleep-wake cycles in healthy human subjects.

*Source: C14 + A1.3.*

Nolan-as-bridge framing slips from inference to assertion in some renderings — the inference that he treats all three because he has identified a unifying signature is supported but not directly stated by Nolan in those terms (per audit catch C6.1/C6.2).

## H_BP10 — Population B Craft Physics (split into structural and empirical)

*Source: Meridian + C9 + A3.*

This hypothesis admits the **structural / empirical discrimination** required by Master Glossary §11. The original H_BP10 conflated the framework's structural prediction with the empirical observation; the audit-corrected form splits them.

### H_BP10a — Frequency-Matching Substrate Coupling (framework-structural)

**Statement.** Substrate-coupling between consciousness and external coherent EM sources requires C9 lens-overlap (frequency matching). The framework predicts this *structurally* — frequency matching matters at the substrate-coupling level. The framework does NOT pre-specify which frequency band is the consciousness-coupling-relevant one.

**Status.** Framework prediction. Defensible; testable via any experimental design that varies frequency-matching while holding other coupling conditions constant.

### H_BP10b — Delta-Range Empirical Convergence (empirically-derived)

**Statement.** The frequency band that empirically appears to enable biological-substrate-to-external-EM coupling is the delta range (~0.5-4 Hz), based on multi-substrate convergence:

- Kobayashi 1999 hippocampal-theta (4-7 Hz) biophoton coupling — direct biological measurement
- Persinger 2016 whole-body biophoton 3.93 Hz discriminative — direct biological measurement
- Akdag 2024 4 Hz PEMF bone-healing — controlled tissue-regeneration experiment
- Szabó 2026 STN delta during action cancellation — direct human cognitive-substrate measurement
- Skywatcher leak (Electric Propulsive Spacecraft profile) 1 Hz refill rate — single-source non-peer-reviewed engineering claim

**Status.** Empirical observation. The framework is *consistent with* this convergence but did not pre-specify the band a priori. The convergence itself is suggestive but admits alternative explanations: biological substrates may have basin-of-attraction at delta for reasons unrelated to consciousness-coupling specifically; metabolic processes in this band shape biophoton emission rates; cross-organism oscillation entrainment may produce convergence on delta without requiring consciousness-coupling structure.

**A71 (anomaly).** Whether the delta-range specificity reflects framework-relevant substrate-coupling structure or biology-basin-of-attraction is the discriminating question. Resolution requires identifying a *non-delta* prediction the framework makes that lands; or identifying a delta-band test where the framework's prediction would diverge from biology-basin-of-attraction predictions.

## H_BP11 — Suppression-Cluster Meta-Prediction (candidate-pattern)

*Source: framework meta-prediction.*

**Statement.** Biophotonics, free-energy / coherent-substrate energy extraction, anti-gravity / EM-gravity coupling, plasma physics — the framework predicts these should cluster in suppression-pattern behavior because they are instances of the same underlying physics (coherent multi-scale substrate dynamics with EM-gravity coupling at the appropriate scales).

**Empirical status (candidate-pattern, not confirmed-pattern).** Documented cases consistent with the prediction: Popp marginalized; Mallove murdered (2004); Eskridge's "anti-gravity discovered 4× independently and each classified" pattern; plasma physicist mortality cluster (~2001-2005).

**Counter-instance pending evaluation (A73).** Skywatcher publishes contact-experiencer-adjacent claims with academically-credentialed signatories (Garry Nolan + 8 others) without disruption. The Electric Propulsive Spacecraft X profile aggregates engineering-spec-level antigravity-research claims without disruption. These are empirical counter-instances to a clean H_BP11 prediction.

**Selection-effect concern (audit catch C5.1).** Suppression-cluster pattern identification has selection-effect risk: cases that fit the pattern get counted as confirmation; cases that don't fit (researchers in unrelated fields who also die early or get marginalized) escape attention. The cluster might be cluster-attribution rather than independent-mechanism evidence.

**Status.** Candidate-pattern. Should NOT be cited as confirmed-pattern in any propagation until A73 is resolved and selection-effect rigor is applied. The framework's structural reasoning that the same underlying physics should be classified together remains valid as a *prediction*; the empirical case that this is observed remains *partial*.

**Tractable test path.** A73 monitoring (12-month Skywatcher tracking horizon); systematic case-collection with selection-effect controls.

## H_BP13 — Biophoton-Riemann Spectral Structure (cross-framework)

*Source: Coherence Principle H_BP1 + NMSI (Lazarev) RON-with-Riemann-zero-indexed-phases.*

**Statement.** If NMSI's RON-as-fundamental-substrate AND H_BP1 (biophotons as inter-stream measurement-event closure mechanism) are both correct, then biophotonic coherence frequencies should exhibit spectral structure matching Montgomery-Dyson GUE statistics (the spacing distribution of Riemann ζ zeros) at the appropriate biological scale. The detected coherent biophoton emission spectrum should show pair correlation function consistent with GUE eigenvalue statistics, scaled by the relevant biological coherence length.

**Empirical status.** Untested. No published study has analyzed biophoton spectral data for GUE-spacing structure.

**Why it is sharp.** Neither framework alone predicts this. Combined, they do. Testable against existing biophoton-laboratory data without requiring new instrumentation — Popp-tradition labs may already have spectral data containing this structure, simply unanalyzed for it. Falsifiable: if the spacing distribution matches Poisson reference (random) or some other heavy-tailed alternative (multifractal, scale-free, log-normal) rather than Wigner-surmise GUE, the cross-framework prediction is falsified, narrowing the framework-overlap region.

**Hedges.** Benfatto et al. 2025 establish biophoton emission is super-Poissonian with heavy tails — non-Poisson floor condition is met, leaving open which structured alternative (GUE among others) best matches. Specific Wigner-surmise discrimination requires the analysis pipeline to be run; ruling out alternatives requires careful sample-size analysis.

**Tractable test path.** Adapted Binder-Simpson pair-correlation methodology + Huang et al. higher-order N-photon correlation formalism + Benfatto multi-method baseline applied to Persinger-lineage spectral data. Test point candidate: 1.094 MHz mesoscopic carrier frequency from Electric Propulsive Spacecraft profile (single specific frequency where GUE-spacing prediction can be evaluated).
