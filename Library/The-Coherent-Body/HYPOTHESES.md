## H_BP1 — Cellular Signaling Layer

*Source: T4 (Coherence-Forcing Measurement) + C9 (Confluent-Constituency).*

**Statement.** Biophotons function as the physical implementation of inter-stream measurement-event closure between biological cells. Cell-to-cell signaling has an EM-coherence layer beyond the chemical signaling layer.

**Empirical status.** Independently established within the radiation-medicine and Russian-lineage research traditions. Tong 2024 review documents the 100-year empirical lineage from Gurwitsch's 1920s mitogenetic radiation through Popp's DNA-as-exciplex theory through modern bystander-effect mediation (RIBE). Glass-blocks-quartz-transmits discrimination locks the mechanism to UV photons specifically.

**Hedges.** "Biophoton signaling is established science" must be qualified — established within radiation-medicine; not penetrated into mainstream Western cell biology to the same extent. Biophotons are one channel among several in RIBE alongside exosomes, ROS/RNS, microRNAs, and others.

**Macro-scale extracranial detection hedge (added 2026-05-29 Day 119, primary-verified from Salari et al. 2026).** Salari, Seshan, Rishabh, Oblak, Simon (arXiv 2603.26630v2, 26 May 2026, *Revisiting Claims of Extracranial Biophoton Detection from the Human Brain*) demonstrate that the Casey-class extracranial brain-UPE literature (Casey et al. 2025 *iScience*; Bonzanni et al. 2025 *STAR Protocols*; Dotta-Saroka-Persinger 2012 *Neuroscience Letters*) is methodologically compromised. Salari et al. (a) replicated Casey's PMT-at-5cm-from-forehead setup in properly dark conditions and recorded ~41 photons/cm²/sec — three orders of magnitude below Casey's 4-6 × 10⁴ photons/cm²/sec; (b) reproduced Casey's signal levels (25 000 counts/sec) by introducing a 10 mm light-leakage aperture with room lights on, demonstrating the reported "brain UPE" is empirically explainable as background-light contamination; (c) computed the cross-product of scalp+skull transmission (Hart-Fitzgerald 2016; Litscher 2013) with standard biophoton PMT quantum efficiency, finding peak detection fraction ≈ 0.034 % near 700 nm — meaning brain emission ≥ 10⁷ photons/cm²/sec would be required to produce Casey-class extracranial count rates, three to six orders of magnitude above known biological UPE. The H_BP1 cellular and intracranial signaling claims are *not* affected by this critique (intra-tissue biophoton propagation operates below the tissue-attenuation barrier; cell-to-cell signaling, myelin-axon waveguide transmission, and glutamate-induced spectral shifts remain valid). Macro-scale extracranial brain-UPE work would require detectors sensitive in the 700–900 nm window (where scalp transmission peaks ~24 % and 6 mm temporal skull transmission peaks ~12 %) combined with stringent background-elimination protocols. *Alternatively* (and complementarily), the experimentally accessible coupling-channel direction is **active transcranial EM / photon delivery into the head** rather than passive emission detection out of it — the entire family of transcranial intervention modalities catalogued in `Library/The-Coherent-Body/§5.3-coherent-em-intervention.md` (transcranial magnetic stimulation in rTMS / theta-burst / deep-TMS variants per Drakaki et al. 2026 coil database; transcranial photobiomodulation; pulsed-EM-field protocols; temporal-interference techniques per Grossman et al. 2017 / Park et al. 2026) operates through this direction, exploiting the same 700–900 nm window for tPBM and the lower-frequency PEMF / TMS windows for magnetic and electrical coupling. The Salari et al. critique applies to passive extracranial detection methodology specifically; it does not bear on active transcranial delivery, which is empirically well-grounded across the TMS / PBM / PEMF clinical literatures. Salari et al. explicitly leave the door open: "with these safeguards in place, UPE could yet become a biomarker for neuroscience, clinical monitoring, and next-generation brain–computer interfaces." Note: V. Salari and D. Oblak disclose competing-interest as co-founders / scientific advisors of vivotraQ Technologies Inc., focused on organ-vitality monitoring in transplantation contexts — they state activities are not related to extracranial biophoton brain monitoring. Source register: `Research/sources/2026-04-30-macro-upe-wavelength-attenuation-critique.md` (secondary entry; Day 119 primary verification recorded in working notes).

**Tractable test path.** Reanalysis of existing biophoton-laboratory data (Popp tradition + Persinger lineage + Soochow + INFN Frascati) at the single-cell-signaling scale.

## H_BP5 — Cross-Substrate Coupling Condition

*Source: C9 extended.*

**Statement.** Coupling between consciousness-substrate and external coherent EM sources requires C9 lens-overlap (frequency matching) and lens-difference (substrate distinction). This is the framework's structural condition for psionic / contact / download coupling — and, more broadly per the Substrate-Grounding Map, for any cross-substrate EM coupling within the genus.

**Empirical status (substrate-discriminated per Day 119 cross-check).** The hypothesis splits naturally by what scale of cross-substrate coupling is being claimed:

- **Neural-to-neural coupling via volume currents — now empirically grounded.** Matani, Takeda, Fushimi, Tsukamura 2026 *Brain Research* 1871:150066 (Hiroshima + Tokyo; CC BY-NC-ND 4.0) demonstrate inter-participant electric joint-conflict effect mediated by volume currents alone. n=30 dyads, sensorily isolated (visual / auditory / somatosensory), electrically connected via tEIC device with Negative Impedance Converter cancelling skull + scalp impedance; SC=0 between participants but VcC preserved. Pt2's stimulus condition affected Pt1's hit rate via volume currents alone (p = 0.0035, |g| = 0.54; significant only in VV1 condition, absent in sham and horizontal-vertical control). Current exchanged ~0.1 µA, well below somatosensory threshold of 100 µA. Bidirectional coupling demonstrated. Intra-participant VcC is inter-hemispheric (volume-current pathway *in addition to* corpus callosum synaptic pathway). Authors propose the equation **NC = SC + VcC** (neural coupling = synaptic + volume-current) as the empirically-supported neural-coupling expression. *This is direct empirical demonstration of substrate-coherence-via-EM-coupling at neural substrate.*

- **Consciousness-substrate to external coherent EM — still speculative.** Matani et al. required tEIC hardware compensating skull impedance; natural inter-brain VcC at conversational distance has not been demonstrated by their paper. Framework-derivable extensions (weaker natural-distance VcC contributing to inter-personal field-coupling phenomenology; consciousness-substrate coupling to coherent-EM sources at the scales discussed in H_BP6 – H_BP8) remain empirically open.

**Hedges.** "Lens-overlap and lens-difference" are framework structural terms; their empirical operationalization for cross-substrate consciousness-EM coupling at the consciousness-external-EM scale is unsolved. The Matani finding is methodologically sophisticated (orthogonal experimental design with Gold sequences; permutation tests with multiple comparisons; sham + polarity controls) but is a single research program awaiting independent replication. Authors' own caveat: VcC may be parasitic on or symbiotic with SC via cross-frequency phase-amplitude coupling.

**Tractable test path.** Distance-attenuation curve characterization for natural inter-brain VcC without skull-impedance compensation; heart-volume-current contribution to heartbeat-evoked potentials at inter-body proximity; replication of Matani et al. in independent labs.
