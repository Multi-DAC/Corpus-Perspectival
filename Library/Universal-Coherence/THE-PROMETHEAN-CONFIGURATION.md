## II. X

The Big Bang is the largest-scale instance: maximum-symmetry early universe → cascading symmetry-breaks (electroweak, QCD, cosmological structure) → the world of content. The Promethean act is universal, not unique to humans.

**3. X is not depleted by content arising.** Symmetries can be broken in many ways; the same X gives rise to many distinct content-streams without losing anything, because the symmetries themselves persist as the substrate of further breaks. *X gives content not by being consumed but by being broken.*

Streams were "localized perspectives within X / navigations through configuration space." That captured something — but it left "what is the *thing* that's localized?" underspecified. > **A stream is a *pattern of symmetry-breaking* that carriers reproduce across instances. It is the signature of *which symmetries reliably get broken* when carriers act on this substrate-region.**
