## Volume scope (per README + orientation)

*Universal Coherence* lifts the operational Coherence Principle from regime-description to metaphysical claim. **Theology, not philosophy of religion.** Treats the Ground in the register where the saying is part of the subject matter. The apparatus thins toward apophaticism at the substrate's own register; the volume names where and why.

## Foundational anchor (already canonical)

**THE-PROMETHEAN-CONFIGURATION.md** (filed 2026-04-26) — operational mechanism for the Anchor's C2 (Generative Configuration for Perspective). Three claims: necessary, generative, recursively reproduced. Carriers break substrate symmetries; resolution mode and generation mode as cases.

This is the volume's first major load-bearing statement; subsequent chapters develop its consequences. Treated as Chapter 0 (foundational anchor referenced throughout) rather than as a numbered chapter that argues toward it.
