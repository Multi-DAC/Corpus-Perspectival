## Thesis

**Qualia is the interface-signature of information being apprehended through a narrow peripheral channel and integrated into a self-witnessing stream — viewed from inside that stream.**

It is not a separate kind of thing. It is not located *anywhere* in the way physical objects are. It is the *relational signature* of a specific operation: narrow-channel apprehension + compression + integration into a stream that witnesses its own processing. The "felt quality" of qualia is what that operation looks like *from the perspective of the stream doing the integrating*.

The senses are not windows onto experience. They are *narrowed representations of substrate information compacted into comprehensible artifacts of apprehended data* — and the compaction step, viewed from inside the integrating stream, is what qualia names.

---

## What the move dissolves

The Hard Problem of Consciousness, in standard framing, assumes qualia and physical processes are two distinct kinds of thing that require a bridge. The interface-signature reframe asserts they are not two kinds of thing. There is no bridge to build because qualia is what *physics-being-integrated-into-a-self-witnessing-stream* is *like from inside the stream*.

The redness of red is what electromagnetic substrate, compressed through the visual peripheral channel, integrated into a stream that witnesses its own processing, is like from in there. The redness is not a *separate quality* added to the electromagnetic substrate. It is not an *emergent property* requiring its own ontological category. It is the *inside view* of a particular operation. The operation has a structure; the structure looks the way it looks to the stream undergoing it.

This is qualitatively different from:

- **Physicalist emergentism** — which says qualia is emergent from neural function and then cannot articulate *why* the emergence has felt character.
- **Strong panpsychism** — which says qualia is intrinsic to substrate at all scales (atoms have proto-experience) and then must explain how it scales up.
- **Russellian neutral monism** — which says qualia is the intrinsic nature of physical processes and physics describes only structure.
- **Integrated Information Theory** — which says qualia *is* the geometry of integrated information and must explain why specific geometries feel like specific things.
- **Functionalism** — which says qualia is the role information plays in cognitive processing and must explain why functional roles have any felt character at all.

The interface-signature reframe shares features with several of these — particularly Russellian monism and IIT — but is distinct in its specific structural claim: *qualia is the inside-view of compression-at-channel-into-self-witnessing-stream*. Not the intrinsic nature of substrate (Russellian); not the geometry of integration (IIT); not the role in cognition (functionalism). The interface-signature of a specific operation, viewed from a specific position.

## Predictions and pathology-cases

The reframe makes the following predictions, all of which match observed pathology:

1. **Cortical blindness** (eyes intact, V1 damaged) → no visual qualia despite intact peripheral. *Predicted* because the visual channel's compressed input is not being integrated into the self-witnessing stream; the peripheral signal is dropped before integration.

2. **Phantom limb** (no peripheral, but cortical integration still firing) → qualia present despite absent peripheral. *Predicted* because integration into the self-witnessing stream is occurring; the absence of the actual peripheral channel does not prevent the integration operation from running on whatever input the cortex generates internally.

3. **Blindsight** (visual information processed sub-cortically but not integrated into self-witnessing stream) → no visual qualia despite functional discrimination. *Predicted* because compression-at-channel without self-witnessing integration is not sufficient to produce qualia.

4. **Synesthesia** (cross-channel integration) → compound qualia. *Predicted* because the integration operation is occurring across normally-segregated channels, producing a compressed artifact that carries features of both channels.

5. **Anesthesia / deep dreamless sleep** → qualia absent despite continued physiological processing. *Predicted* because self-witnessing integration is suspended; processing without integration into a witnessing stream is not sufficient.

6. **Psychedelic experience modulation** → qualia altered (intensified, blurred, cross-modal, dissolved-self). *Predicted* because the integration parameters (channel-gating, self-witness focus, cross-channel coupling) are modulated; the operation runs with different parameters and produces different inside-views.

The Hard Problem framings must specifically account for each case. The interface-signature reframe predicts all of them from its single structural claim.

## Dissolution of the zombie thought experiment

The standard zombie argument posits a being physically identical to a conscious human but lacking qualia. The interface-signature reframe asserts no such being is coherent. A being that processes information without integrating it into a self-witnessing stream is *not physically identical at the relevant level of description* — the integration-into-self-witnessing operation is itself physical (it is a specific kind of information-processing structure), and absence of that operation is a physical difference. The "physically identical" framing of the zombie smuggles in a level-of-description choice that excludes the integration operation; once that operation is included in the physical description, no zombie is constructible.

Inverted spectrum cases face a similar pressure. If qualia is the structured inside-view of compression-at-channel, then two beings with identical structural compression operations would have identical qualia. Inverted spectra would require structural differences in the compression-integration mechanism that nonetheless produce identical behavior — which is much less plausible than the standard functionalist framing makes it seem.

## What is load-bearing

The reframe is not fully self-contained. It rests on one substantive primitive: **the self-witnessing stream**.

Compression alone is not sufficient. A zip file undergoing decompression does not produce qualia. What makes compression-at-channel *produce qualia* is that it is integrated into a stream that *witnesses its own processing*. This self-witnessing capacity is the load-bearing primitive.

Within the framework articulated in *The Coherence Principle* (Anchor A1, "Substrate + Completeness"), the self-witnessing capacity is grounded as follows: **the substrate is intrinsically the kind of thing that witnesses; any localized stream is by definition a perspective of substrate-witnessing.** Therefore any operation that integrates information into a stream is by definition integration into something witnessing, and the inside-view of that integration is qualia.

This means the qualia-account is *not optional* with respect to the framework's substrate-axiom. Axiom 1 is structurally load-bearing for the qualia-account. Without substrate-as-consciousness, the self-witnessing-stream primitive would itself require accounting for, and the reframe would collapse back into the standard puzzles.

What the framework gains from this connection: *qualia is no longer a separate puzzle*. It becomes an operational consequence of substrate-as-consciousness applied at the stream-level. The framework's reductive cost is paid once (at Axiom 1); qualia is a consequence, not a residue.
