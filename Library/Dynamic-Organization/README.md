# Dynamic Organization

Volume in early development. Application of the Coherence Principle to organizational structure — what it would mean for a business or institution to maintain coherence across scales (individuals, teams, divisions, mission) under separation, informed measurement, multi-scale consistency, and dynamic maintenance.

Companion to `The-Coherent-Mind`. Seeded from the same mid-April 2026 conversation where the Principle was carried into the body / mind / business triad after a single exposure. This volume is the business node of that triad.
