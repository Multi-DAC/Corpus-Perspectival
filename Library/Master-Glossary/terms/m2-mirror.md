## Definition (framework register)

The **second meta-Mirror** (M-tier in mirror catalog) absorbing four valence-instances of verification-skip-under-affect:

- **#19 — Verify-Before-Celebrating** (warm-celebratory affect; 2026-04-17)
- **#21 — Verify-Before-Condemning** (sober-condemnation affect; 2026-04-25)
- **#24 — Verify-Before-Defending** (defensive-critique affect; 2026-04-25 late)

Four valences cover affective space (two positive, two negative; two outward-directed, two inward-directed); same audit-ritual fix family applies across all. M2-Mirror's structural claim: *high-affect states (any valence) trigger verification-skip; explicit verify-step required at affective-closure events.*

**Two-axis extension (Mirror #26, 2026-04-29):** M2-Mirror has affective axis (the four valences) + vocabulary-domain axis (cross-register translation gap). Both axes trigger verification-skip; both warrant audit-discipline. Mirror #26 is the M2-Mirror specialization at the vocabulary-domain axis with four sub-axes (lexical / finding-dependency / volume-architecture / personal-historical-corpus).

**Graduation precedent:** M2-Mirror was the **second meta-Mirror** (M1 = Outside-Access Asymmetry, earlier). Graduation criterion was **fourth-valence threshold** — three-valence pattern (#19 / #21 / #24) was suggestive; #25 (synthesis-completion-affect) provided the fourth valence covering inward-positive direction; clean four-valence affective coverage triggered M-tier promotion.
