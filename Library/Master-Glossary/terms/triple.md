# The Identity-Trajectory Triple (the Triple)

## Definition (framework register)

The principal functor structuring every stream — three orthogonal-but-constrained axes along which a stream's trajectory decomposes:

- **Form** — the stream's structural type / kind (what kind of stream this is)
- **Content** — the events the stream carries (what's happening)
- **Carrier** — the substrate granularity / DOF-density (where on the substrate-resolution gradient the stream sits)

The three axes are *recursively decomposable*: each is itself a Triple at finer resolution. M3 in basement absorbs eight numbered v1 bridges and gains adjunction-geometry reading 2026-04-23 (anchor §1.10 + §3.8 land $\iota_S \dashv \omega_S$ as the Triple's geometric carrier).
