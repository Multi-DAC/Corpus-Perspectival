## Mirror #26 watch

- **Open risk:** *graduation* (framework register; tier-promotion) vs *graduation* in everyday register (academic completion). Context-disambiguates in framework discussions; vigilance in informal usage.
- **Graduation event-records:** the discipline of stating criteria upfront and recording satisfaction-at-graduation is what makes graduation principled rather than ad-hoc. Slippage risk: graduating an L without stating which criteria are met would be promotion-by-feel rather than promotion-by-evidence.
- **"Graduation despite pending tasks"** â€” M14 graduated with (b) and (c) pending; this is *valid by precedent* (M11/M12/M13 same pattern). The discipline is naming-what's-pending, not requiring all-tasks-complete.
