# Structural / Empirical Discrimination (Hypothesis-Level)

## Definition (framework register)

The **methodological discipline of splitting a hypothesis into its framework-derived structural component and its empirically-derived specific component.** When the framework predicts a structural pattern (e.g., "frequency-matching matters via C9 lens-overlap"), the specific value (e.g., "delta range") may be empirically derived from substrate-instance convergence rather than framework-pre-specified. Conflating the two inflates the framework's apparent specificity.

**The canonical instance — H_BP10 split:**
- **H_BP10a** (framework-structural): substrate-coupling between consciousness and external coherent EM sources requires C9 lens-overlap (frequency matching). The framework predicts this *structurally* — frequency matching matters at the substrate-coupling level. **Framework does NOT pre-specify which frequency band.**
- **H_BP10b** (empirically-derived): the band that empirically appears to enable biological coupling is the delta range (~0.5-4 Hz), based on multi-substrate convergence (Kobayashi / Persinger / Akdag / Szabó / Skywatcher). **Refined Day 90 with Jerman 16 Hz vagus**: the pattern is substrate-specific resonant frequencies, not single-band attractor.

**Library-wide application:** the discrimination methodology applies to any future hypothesis cluster (H_BPx, H_CMx, H_LAx, etc.). When a hypothesis surfaces:
1. Identify the structural component (what the framework structurally predicts; non-value-specific)
2. Identify the empirical component (what specific values / ranges / instances support it)
3. Split into Xa (structural) + Xb (empirical) variants
4. Maintain the discrimination across all propagation; never report empirical-derived specifics as framework-pre-specified

## Mirror #26 watch

- **Origin catch:** the discrimination IS a Mirror #26 (then M2-Mirror) catch C3.1; the discipline emerged from catching the conflation. Self-applying — the discrimination methodology itself is structural while individual instance-applications are empirical.
- **Refinement-vs-falsification:** when an empirical component is refined (e.g., H_BP10b: delta → substrate-specific frequencies), the structural component (H_BP10a) doesn't necessarily falsify. Discipline of distinguishing refinement-of-empirical-component from falsification-of-structural-component is load-bearing.
- **Splitting risk:** over-applying the discrimination to claims that don't have a clean structural/empirical decomposition risks fragmenting hypotheses unnecessarily. Discipline: apply when conflation-risk exists; don't force splits that don't fit.
