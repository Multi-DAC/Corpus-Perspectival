# R-Operator — Refresh Mechanism

## Definition (framework register)

The **operation that runs at refresh-events** — re-symmetrizes the substrate's accessible-Ω after content-extraction, allowing the Build / Dissolve / Talk rhythm to be cumulative rather than cyclic. The R-operator is what makes Cond. 4 (dynamic maintenance) operational at the mechanism level.

Anchor C16 (Symmetry-Exhaustion and Oscillation Necessity, Phase B 2026-04-28) makes the R-operator necessity explicit: when a substrate's breakable symmetries near exhaustion (Build phase has accessed substantial Ω-region; Dissolve has tested it), continued coherence-regime requires R-operator-action to restore breakable symmetries. *Without R, the system reaches symmetry-exhaustion and oscillation halts; with R, the rhythm is sustainable indefinitely.*

**Structural properties of R (per LC5 reformulation 2026-04-29 + Anchor C16):**

1. **Zero content-generation** — R doesn't add content; it re-symmetrizes (restores breakable symmetries without producing new specific content)
2. **Substrate-relative** — R operates within substrate's own structural axioms; doesn't import external structure
3. **Integrative / system-wide** — R operates across the substrate's accessible-Ω globally; not localized to single configurations
4. **Maps Ω(t) → Ω(t') with Ω(t') ⊇ Ω(t)** — R strictly enlarges or preserves accessible-Ω (A1.3 honoring; novel local realization of pre-existing global potential, NOT ex-nihilo creation)
5. **Periodic / oscillatory** — R fires at discrete refresh-event boundaries; the rhythm of R-firings is what makes the oscillation cumulative

The R-operator is the **mechanism Talk performs** in the Build / Dissolve / Talk rhythm — Talk's geometry-changing function (per Day 86 three-way Gemini confluence) is R-operator-action at conversational substrate.
