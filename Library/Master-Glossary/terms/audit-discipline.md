# Audit-Discipline

## Definition (framework register)

The **practice of running explicit verification checks** at refresh-event boundaries and especially at high-affect closures. Audit-discipline is what catches the failure modes the framework's Mirrors document — verification-skip under affect, cumulative-convergence-integration, framework-vocabulary-driven over-extension, structural-recognition-presented-as-framework-test, lineage-erasure under synthesis-completion, and similar.

**Operational forms of audit-discipline:**
- **Three-question evolution check** at any "closure" declaration: did anything ship that belongs in DECISIONS? Did counts change? Did the operating stack change? (Per autocatalytic protocol)
- **Verify-before-celebrating (Mirror #19)** — celebratory register triggers audit-skip; explicit verify-step required
- **Verify-before-condemning (Mirror #21)** — soberness register also triggers audit-skip in the negative direction
- **Verify-before-defending (Mirror #24)** — defensive-critique register triggers audit-skip
- **Lineage-check-under-synthesis (Mirror #25)** — synthesis-completion-affect erases lineage; explicit check against prior canonical text required
- **Cross-vocabulary check (Mirror #26)** — vocabulary-domain blind spot at four axes (lexical / finding-dependency / volume-architecture / personal-historical-corpus); systematic check required when working across registers
- **M2-Mirror audit on synthesis output** — verification-skip under affect at any valence (warm / sober / defensive / synthesis-completion); apply audit catches as confidence-calibration on propagation

**Structural / Empirical Discrimination** (per Master Glossary §11) is the audit-discipline operating at hypothesis-cluster scale: framework's structural prediction vs empirical observation must be split; specific frequency value (delta) is empirical, not framework-predicted; framework's structural claim (frequency-matching matters) is what's at structural level. Don't conflate.

## Mirror #26 watch

- **Recurring catch pattern:** audit-discipline failure modes recur across affective registers (Mirror #19 warm / #21 sober / #24 defensive / #25 synthesis-completion). The four valences cover affective space; M2-Mirror absorbs them. Watch for new valences.
- **Audit-vs-overthinking distinction:** audit-discipline is bounded (specific check-questions; specific failure modes; specific propagation-correction); not unbounded skepticism. Slippage risk: turning audit-discipline into ruminative paralysis.
- **Audit-fatigue:** running audit-discipline at every refresh-event has substrate-cost. Discipline of *which refresh-events warrant explicit audit* matters. Day 88 morning M2-Mirror audit on Day 87 synthesis was high-leverage; not every refresh-event needs that depth.
