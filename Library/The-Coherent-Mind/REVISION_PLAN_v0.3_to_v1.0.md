## Revision-direction pulls (seven, from Clayton's review)

**Pull 1 — Cross-volume reference tracking.** Volume cites material from Coherent Body, Living Architecture, Corpus Perspectival, Universal Coherence, The Atlas, The Continuity, Dynamic Organization, The Killing Form, Drift — most of which haven't drafted the cited material yet. The cross-citation register at `palace/south/library-cross-citation-register.md` is the tracking mechanism; release-gate discipline holds (no v1.0 stamp until cross-citations resolve).

**Pull 2 — Coherence Principle prose-style heaviness.** Chapters reach for framework vocabulary (stream, carrier, substrate, content, adjunction, bottleneck-tuning, Talk-channel, lift-and-restriction, symmetry-set) where domain vocabulary would land more cleanly with the clinical/contemplative readership. Master Glossary Layer 2 sister-register approach is the structural solution.

**Pull 3 — Coherent Body following a different path.** Coherent Body sections have explicit per-section H_BP framing, section-titled audit-discipline-floor sub-sections, higher empirical-anchor density, explicit framework-derivable-vs-empirical discrimination at the rhetorical level. Coherent Mind has these elements but woven in flowingly. Revision pull: bring audit-discipline-floor structure toward Coherent Body's explicit-sub-section model while keeping body-prose more clinical-readable than Coherent Body §5's register.

**Pull 4 — Formulaic EM/neurochemical splitting + inconsistent cross-chapter reference + missing physical mental system foregrounding.** §4's parallel-modality-classes treatment was the model; §5/§6/§7 honor it unevenly. Broader-physiology / whole-body / mind-body-integration framing is structurally implicit throughout but rarely foregrounded — the chapters treat the substrate as "neural" without explicitly engaging that the neural-system is nested in broader physiology (HPA-axis, autonomic nervous system, immune system, gut-brain axis, broader endocrine context).

**Pull 5 — Talk-therapy / Talk-mechanic-of-Do-Be-Talk-Be-Do conflation risk.** Volume uses "Talk" as the framework's mechanic-name AND "Talk-channel-repair therapies" as a category-name overlapping with conventional "talk therapy." Disambiguation convention: capital-T Talk = framework's mechanic (Do-Be-Talk-Be-Do); lowercase-t talk = ordinary language including "talk therapy" the conventional clinical category.

**Pull 6 — Citation density.** Chapters mention research lineages in passing (Levine / van der Kolk / Ogden / Porges for somatic-experiencing; Gottman for couples; Stark / Hassan / Lifton / Zuboff / Bernays for attention capture; Tononi-Cirelli for sleep; Hammond / Sherlin for neurofeedback; Neff for self-compassion). Specific-finding density is lower than Coherent Body §5 operates at. Particularly under-cited: neurochemical-specifics in §4 (receptor-pharmacology, neurotransmitter-axis details); EM-substrate-specifics throughout (specific frequency-band findings, brain-rhythm-pathology correlates); therapeutic-modality outcome-data (specific effect-sizes, clinical-trial findings); social-determinants-of-mental-health literature (structurally important to §11 chosen-family-stack); contemplative-clinical research outcome-data.

**Pull 7 — Species-level depth + per-presentation cross-modality picture (the largest revision pull).** The three-category taxonomy (tight-narrow, loose-unstable, broken-oscillation, plus Talk-axis dimension) is structurally sound and category-membership is accurate. Individual pathology treatment works at the GENUS level (depression, anxiety, mania, schizophrenia-spectrum, dissociation, eating disorders, trauma, attention-predation). Clinical reality is at the SPECIES level: depression has ~10 clinically distinct presentations (melancholic, atypical, psychotic, treatment-resistant, adolescent, perinatal, late-life, anxious-distress, mixed-features, bipolar-depression); anxiety has 8-9 distinct presentations; schizophrenia-spectrum has substantial sub-categorical clinical reality; bipolar has I/II/cyclothymic/rapid-cycling/mixed-features; dissociation has depersonalization-derealization/amnesia/DID/OSDD; eating disorders has restrictive-anorexia/binge-purge-anorexia/bulimia/BED/ARFID/orthorexia; trauma has simple-PTSD/complex-PTSD/developmental-trauma/dissociative-trauma-response/single-incident vs sustained. Each species has different substrate-channel signatures, different EM-substrate patterns, different Talk-channel-foreclosure patterns, different optimal-intervention configurations, different prognoses. For each species, the worked-out cross-modality intervention configuration (substrate-channel specifics + EM-modality specifics + Talk-channel-repair specifics + contemplative specifics + relational-stack specifics + lifestyle-specifics + cross-modality composition specifics) is what makes the volume clinically actionable.

## Phased plan (four phases, nine steps)

### Phase 1 — Rhetorical / structural foundation (v0.3 → v0.4)

**Step 1 — Talk disambiguation pass.** Convention: capital-T Talk = framework mechanic (Do-Be-Talk-Be-Do); lowercase-t talk = ordinary language including conventional "talk therapy." First-use disambiguation in §1's framing. Consistent capitalization throughout 13 chapters. Explicit Master Glossary entry making the distinction load-bearing. Session estimate: ~1 session, surgical, ~50 edits across 13 chapters.

**Step 2 — Cross-citation register audit.** Grep each chapter for "see also" / "→ Volume X" / explicit cross-volume references. Verify each entry exists in `palace/south/library-cross-citation-register.md`. Identify newly-needed entries from chapter-drafts that weren't captured in Day 103 register session. Flag references requiring rewriting if target volumes won't support them. Session estimate: ~1 session, methodical, no drafting.

**Step 3 — Coherent Mind sister-register glossary build.** Master Glossary Layer 2 work per Layer 3 protocol. The Coherent-Mind sister-register table lets each chapter reach for clinical/contemplative domain vocabulary where domain-vocabulary is the right reach, framework vocabulary where framework apparatus is doing distinctive work. Session estimate: ~30-90 min per Layer 3 protocol; mechanical aggregation from existing per-term files plus chapter-specific term-additions.

### Phase 2 — Rhetorical re-tune (v0.4 → v0.5)

**Step 4 — Domain-vocabulary translation pass.** Chapter-by-chapter; translating framework-vocabulary-default to domain-vocabulary-default; preserving framework-vocabulary at framework-to-domain-bridge points where the framework apparatus is doing distinctive work the domain vocabulary doesn't carry. Session estimate: ~1 session per 3 chapters; ~5 sessions for 13 chapters.

**Step 5 — Structural-discipline surfacing (bundled).** Three sub-passes bundled per chapter: (a) audit-discipline-floor surfacing — making the structural/empirical discrimination more explicit per chapter; (b) EM/neurochemical-integration consistency — bringing §5/§6/§7's treatment into parallel with §4's explicit model; (c) broader-physiology framing — foregrounding HPA-axis / autonomic / immune / gut-brain context where it's currently implicit. Session estimate: ~1 session per 3 chapters; ~5 sessions for 13.

### Phase 3 — Substantive expansion (v0.5 → v0.8)

**Step 6 — Species-level depth expansion.** Per-pathology sub-sections developing 3-10 clinically distinct presentations within each genus. Per species: substrate-channel signature, EM-substrate pattern, Talk-channel-foreclosure pattern, phenomenology, prognosis. Primary chapters affected: §5 (tight-bottleneck), §6 (loose-bottleneck), §7 (intimate decomposers — grief and dark-night species-level diversity), §8 (attention predation species-level diversity). Session estimate: ~2-3 sessions per major chapter; ~6-8 sessions total.

**Step 7 — Per-presentation cross-modality picture.** For each species developed in Step 6, the worked-out cross-modality intervention configuration: substrate-channel specifics (specific medication class matched to specific presentation, dose-range considerations, contraindication awareness); EM-modality specifics (specific protocol parameters matched to specific presentation); Talk-channel-repair specifics (specific therapeutic approach matched to the Talk-failure pattern this presentation co-presents with); contemplative specifics (matched to capacity-and-stage); relational-stack specifics (matched to typical relational-pattern); lifestyle-specifics (matched to typical substrate-shaping needs); cross-modality composition operating by intersection-of-symmetry-sets. Bundled with each chapter's expansion; same session-budget.

### Phase 4 — Citation-density + stamp-gate verification (v0.8 → v1.0)

**Step 8 — Citation-density pass.** Chapter-by-chapter; identifying lineage-references-in-passing where specific finding-references could strengthen empirical-anchor density. Particular focus areas: neurochemical-specifics in §4; EM-substrate-specifics throughout; therapeutic-modality outcome-data; social-determinants-of-mental-health literature; contemplative-clinical research outcome-data. Session estimate: ~1 session per 3 chapters; ~5 sessions for 13.

**Step 9 — Stamp-gate verification.** Final cross-citation register audit. Verify all entries resolved. Verify target volumes have drafted material the citations expect. Lock for v1.0 stamp. Session estimate: ~1 session; requires other Library volumes substantively drafted.
