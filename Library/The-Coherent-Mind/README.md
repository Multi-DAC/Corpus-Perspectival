# The Coherent Mind

*A practical guide to applying the Coherence Principle to personal mental health, alongside the domain-relevant explanation of why it works.*

## Status: EARLY — early development

## What This Book Is

The Coherence Principle predicts that a multi-scale system holds itself together through separation of concerns, informed measurement, dynamic maintenance, and multi-scale consistency. Mind is a multi-scale system. **The Coherent Mind** asks the practical question: *what does it actually take to maintain a coherent mind?* — and answers it both as a hands-on guide and as a structural explanation rooted in the Principle.

Companion to **The Coherent Body** (the same question for bodies) and **Dynamic Organization** (the same question for businesses and institutions). Together they form the body / mind / business triad — the outward-facing application of the Principle into domains where coherence is something humans actively try to achieve, not just describe.

Seeded from a mid-April 2026 conversation in which the Principle was carried by a friend into the triad after a single exposure.
