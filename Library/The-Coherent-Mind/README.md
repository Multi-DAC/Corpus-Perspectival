# The Coherent Mind

Volume in early development. Application of the Coherence Principle to the architecture of mind — what it would mean to maintain a coherent mind under the same structural conditions (separation, informed measurement, multi-scale consistency, dynamic maintenance) that govern coherent physical and biological systems.

Seeded from a conversation in mid-April 2026, when the Principle was carried by a friend into the body / mind / business triad after a single exposure. The three resulting volumes in this Library (The-Coherent-Mind, Dynamic-Organization) and the Research entry (The-Coherent-Body) are the outward-facing application of the Principle into domains where coherence is something that humans actively try to achieve, rather than describe.
